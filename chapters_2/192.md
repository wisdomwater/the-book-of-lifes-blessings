## 192 - The Fragrance That Remains

> *No act of love is forgotten*  
> *What is offered in mercy lives on*

I saw a quiet light  
rising in the early dawn.

It moved gently upward,  
like the fragrance of rain  
lifting from the earth after night.

Within it were many small things—  
a whispered prayer,  
a hand extended in kindness,  
a tear offered in silence,  
a burden carried for another.

None appeared large.

None demanded to be seen.

And yet,  
as they rose,  
they became luminous.

The light gathered them  
without losing even the smallest one.

Nothing disappeared.

Each offering remained alive,  
held within a vast and tender remembrance.

And beyond the rising light,  
there was a brightness  
too deep for words—  
clear,  
peaceful,  
welcoming all that had been given in love.

Even the earth itself  
seemed to breathe with hope,  
as if all things were slowly being restored  
to their first beauty.

And I understood:

What is done in love  
is never lost.

It continues—  
quietly,  
radiantly—  
within the heart of God.

#### Living the Vision

Sometimes your small acts  
seem to vanish unnoticed.

A prayer spoken softly.  
A kindness no one remembers.  
A sacrifice offered quietly  
without recognition.

You may wonder  
whether any of it matters.

But there may be  
a deeper truth  
already holding these things.

You may notice it  
in the quiet peace  
that sometimes follows an honest act of love.

Not reward.  
Not certainty.

Just a gentle sense  
that something real  
has been given.

You are not asked  
to make your life extraordinary.

Only to remain open  
to love in small and faithful ways.

A listening presence.  
A merciful response.  
A sincere prayer  
spoken in weariness.

None of these are forgotten.

Something in you  
already knows this.

And when you offer yourself  
even gently,  
even imperfectly,  
your life begins to carry  
a quiet fragrance—  
one that lingers  
far beyond what you can see.
