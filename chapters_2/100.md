## 100 - The Living Field

> *What is planted within you is alive*  
> *It responds to every gentle tending*

I saw a wide field  
stretching quietly beneath the morning light.

The soil was deep—  
not empty,  
but waiting.

Beneath the surface,  
something lived—  
seeds already sown,  
hidden,  
held.

At first,  
the ground seemed still.

Then I noticed  
small movements—  
soft openings  
where life was beginning  
to rise.

A presence moved through the field—  
slowly,  
with care.

It did not force the earth.

It touched it—  
turning where it was firm,  
clearing where it was crowded,  
making space  
for what could grow.

Nothing was rushed.

Nothing was neglected.

Each place was tended  
as it was ready.

And where the ground opened,  
light entered.

Where light entered,  
growth followed.

The field responded—  
not by striving,  
but by becoming  
what it was always meant to be.

And I saw  
that everything needed  
was already present—  
waiting  
to be nurtured  
into life.

#### Living the Vision

There are times when  
you wonder  
what is forming within you—

what is growing,  
what is taking root,  
what is quietly shaping  
your life.

You may not always see it clearly.

But something in you  
is always responding  
to what you give attention to.

You may begin  
with simple noticing.

A thought you return to.  
A choice you make.  
A small act of care.

These are like seeds  
being tended.

You do not need  
to control the whole field.

Only to remain present  
to what is before you.

To soften what feels hard.  
To clear what feels crowded.  
To allow space  
for what is good to rise.

This work is gentle.

It unfolds over time.

And you are not alone in it.

Something greater  
moves with you—  
quietly guiding,  
quietly sustaining.

And as you continue,  
you may find  
that what once felt hidden  
begins to show itself—  
not all at once,  
but enough  
to keep you walking.
