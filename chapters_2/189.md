## 189 - The Fire That Gathers

> *What is seen in light can return to life*  
> *What is scattered can be drawn together*

I saw a quiet flame  
burning at the center of a wide space.

It did not rage.  
It did not consume.

It burned steadily—  
clear,  
focused,  
alive.

Around it,  
small fragments lay scattered—  
pieces that had drifted apart,  
light as ash,  
without place.

A faint wind moved through them,  
restless,  
unsettled—  
carrying them here and there  
without rest.

But as the flame remained,  
something began to change.

The scattered pieces  
were gently drawn inward—  
not forced,  
not gathered by hand,  
but drawn  
as if they recognized  
their home.

The wind softened.

The fragments settled.

And as they came near the flame,  
they were no longer scattered.

They became whole again—  
not as they were before,  
but renewed,  
held together  
by a quiet warmth.

Nothing was lost.

Nothing was rejected.

All that could live  
was gathered.

And I understood:

There is a fire  
that does not destroy—  
it reveals,  
and draws back  
what has been scattered.

#### Living the Vision

You feel pulled in many directions.

Thoughts that do not settle.  
Feelings that shift and scatter.  
A quiet sense  
of being divided within.

It can feel like something  
has come apart.

And in those moments,  
you may want to hide—  
to step back from what feels too much,  
to wait until everything feels whole again.

But there may be  
a gentle presence  
already near.

You may notice it  
in a quiet awareness—  
a warmth that does not leave,  
even when everything else feels uncertain.

It does not demand  
that you gather yourself.

It simply remains.

And as you become aware of it,  
even briefly,  
something begins to shift.

You are not asked  
to bring everything back together  
on your own.

Only to stay near  
what is steady.

And in that nearness,  
what feels scattered  
may begin to return—  
piece by piece,  
without force,  
held by something  
that does not waver.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Gather me in Your light  
>
> **Exhale:** Make me whole again
