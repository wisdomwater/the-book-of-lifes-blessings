## 145 - The Flame That Remains

> *You do not need to fight the darkness*  
> *You only need to remain in the flame*

I saw a presence  
standing in quiet strength.

Around it,  
a circle of fire—  
not consuming,  
but alive,  
moving with a steady rhythm.

Its light did not flicker  
with the surrounding shadows.

It remained.

Clear.  
Untroubled.

From within it,  
brightness moved like lightning—  
not harsh,  
but sudden in its clarity,  
revealing what had been hidden.

The darkness did not resist it.

It simply could not remain  
where the light was present.

And I saw  
that this fire  
did not chase anything away.

It did not argue.  
It did not strain.

It simply burned—  
faithful to its nature.

And in that quiet constancy,  
everything false  
lost its hold.

The light did not grow louder.

It grew deeper.

And I understood  
that what is joined to this flame  
does not need to overcome.

It is already  
held in what cannot be undone.

#### Living the Vision

You feel the pull  
of what is not life-giving.

Old thoughts.  
Familiar patterns.  
A heaviness that gathers quietly.

You may feel  
the need to fight it—  
to push it away,  
to overcome it by effort.

But there is another way.

A turning.

Not toward the struggle,  
but toward what is true.

You may begin  
with something simple—  
a breath,  
a word of prayer,  
a small act  
that aligns with what you know is good.

You do not need  
to force the darkness out.

You only need  
to remain near the light.

And as you do,  
something begins to change.

What once felt strong  
loses its weight.

What once felt urgent  
softens.

You are not carrying  
the work of transformation alone.

You are being drawn  
into it.

Stay close  
to what is clear,  
to what is life-giving,  
to what quietly endures.

That is enough.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Keep me near Your flame  
>
> **Exhale:** I remain in Your light
