## 99 - The Light That Knows

> *Nothing true is ever lost*  
> *All things rest within a deeper seeing*

I saw a quiet light  
resting over all things.

It did not move quickly.  
It did not search.

It simply remained—  
clear,  
steady—  
like a presence  
that already knew.

Where it touched,  
nothing was exposed harshly.

Instead,  
things became gently visible—  
as though they had always been seen,  
only now  
without shadow.

There was no urgency in it.  
No demand.

Only a still awareness  
holding everything  
in place.

Even what was hidden  
was not forced into the open.

It rested there—  
known,  
held,  
without fear.

And I saw  
that this light  
did not separate.

It gathered.

What had been scattered  
began to settle.

What had been uncertain  
found quiet ground.

Nothing was lost.

Nothing overlooked.

All was held  
within a knowing  
that did not judge in haste,  
but restored  
with perfect clarity.

And the light remained—  
unchanging,  
patient—  
like a truth  
that does not fade.

#### Living the Vision

You wonder what is seen—

what is known  
beneath the surface of your life.

You may feel  
the desire to hide,  
or to explain,  
or to make sense of everything  
before it is revealed.

But there is a deeper way  
of being known.

You may sense it  
in quiet moments—  
a gentle awareness  
that nothing in you  
is truly hidden.

Not in a way  
that brings fear—  
but in a way  
that invites honesty.

You are already seen.

Fully.

And within that seeing,  
you are not rejected.

You are held.

You may begin  
to rest here—  
to allow yourself  
to be known  
without needing to defend  
or conceal.

And as you do,  
something begins to settle.

Your actions become clearer.  
Your intentions soften.  
Your life aligns  
more easily with what is true.

You do not need  
to uncover everything at once.

Only to remain open  
to the light  
that already knows you.
