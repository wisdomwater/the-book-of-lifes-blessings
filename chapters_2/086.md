## 86 - The Gathering of Light

> *You are not walking alone*  
> *A quiet multitude surrounds you*

I saw a soft white cloud  
resting in stillness.

It did not drift.

It remained—  
gentle,  
luminous,  
full.

Within it,  
there was presence.

Not distant,  
not separate—  
but near,  
like a quiet nearness  
that could be felt  
more than seen.

Forms moved within the light—  
not hurried,  
not striving—  
but carried.

Each one held  
in the same calm radiance.

There was no division there.  
No distance between them.

Only a shared life—  
like many lights  
gathered into one brightness.

And beneath it all,  
a sense of forward movement—  
not rushing,  
but certain.

Like a current  
that does not turn back.

The cloud did not call out loudly.

It simply remained—  
open,  
receiving,  
welcoming.

And I saw  
that it had always been forming—  
quietly gathering  
those who turned toward the light.

Nothing was lost.

Nothing forgotten.

All was held  
within a peace  
that did not fade.

#### Living the Vision

You feel alone in your path—

as if your questions,  
your longing,  
your quiet turning  
are yours alone to carry.

But there is something deeper  
that you may begin to notice.

A sense  
that you are not the first  
to walk this way.

That others have known  
this same longing,  
this same reaching,  
this same quiet trust.

You may feel it  
in small ways—  
a reassurance,  
a steadiness,  
a sense of being accompanied  
even when no one is near.

This is not something  
you need to create.

It is something  
you are already within.

A life that stretches  
beyond what you can see—  
holding you,  
moving with you,  
gently guiding forward.

You do not need  
to rush the journey.

Only to remain  
within this quiet movement—  
turning,  
again and again,  
toward what is true.

And trusting  
that each step  
is already held  
within something greater.
