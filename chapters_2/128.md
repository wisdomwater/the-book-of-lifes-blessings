## 128 - The Fire That Makes Whole

> *What is false cannot remain in His presence*  
> *What is true is strengthened and made clear*

I saw a figure  
standing in quiet strength.

Its presence was steady—  
not harsh,  
not hurried—  
but unyielding in a way  
that felt deeply sure.

Its face held a kind of fire—  
not wild,  
but clear,  
like a flame that knows  
exactly what it is.

Nothing uncertain  
remained near it.

What was heavy  
began to loosen.  
What was tangled  
began to separate.

Not by force,  
but by a quiet revealing—  
as though the fire  
made things visible  
as they truly were.

And I saw  
that what could not remain  
simply fell away—  
not cast out in anger,  
but unable to stay  
where truth was present.

Around this figure  
was a kind of strength—  
like a garment  
woven of something enduring.

Nothing could bend it.  
Nothing could distort it.

It held its place  
in calm certainty.

And I understood  
that this fire  
was not against life—  
it was for it.

It did not seek to destroy,  
but to make whole  
what had been divided.

#### Living the Vision

Something in you  
is brought into the light.

A truth you had avoided.  
A pattern you had excused.  
A place where something  
no longer feels aligned.

It may feel uncomfortable—  
even exposing.

You may want  
to turn away,  
to soften it,  
to delay what is being seen.

But there is another way  
to meet this moment.

With a quiet willingness  
to remain.

To let what is true  
be true  
without rushing to defend yourself.

You may notice  
that what feels like pressure  
is also clarity—  
a kind of inner knowing  
that does not accuse,  
but reveals.

This is not rejection.

It is a deeper care  
that refuses to leave you  
in what diminishes you.

You do not need  
to fix everything at once.

Only to allow  
what is being shown  
to come into the open.

And as you do,  
something begins to change—  
not through force,  
but through honesty  
that makes space  
for what is real to grow.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Make me willing to see  
>
> **Exhale:** Let truth make me whole
