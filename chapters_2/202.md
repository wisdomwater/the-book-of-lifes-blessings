## 202 - The Song Through Wounding

> *What is offered in love is never lost*  
> *Even suffering can become music in God*

I saw a radiant sea  
stretching beneath the light.

It was clear as crystal,  
yet brighter than water—  
still,  
luminous,  
filled with living peace.

And standing upon it  
were shining souls  
clothed in deep red garments  
woven with light.

Their clothing glimmered softly,  
as though every sorrow they had carried  
had been transformed  
into beauty.

Upon their heads  
rested crowns of warm gold,  
encircled by mirrors  
that reflected light in every direction.

In their hands  
they carried transparent branches,  
clear as flowing water.

And within those branches  
small reflections appeared—  
acts of courage,  
moments of mercy,  
hidden faithfulness  
made visible in light.

Then a song began.

Not triumphant in the way of earthly victory,  
but deep,  
tender,  
full of peace.

The song seemed to rise  
from every wound  
that had been carried in love.

Nothing painful was wasted.

Nothing faithful had disappeared.

What once had seemed like loss  
had become praise.

And I understood:

Love offered through suffering  
does not fall into silence.

It becomes part  
of a deeper song.

#### Living the Vision

Faithfulness costs you something.

A misunderstanding.  
A loss.  
A quiet endurance  
no one else fully sees.

You may wonder  
whether the pain carries any meaning,  
whether what you are holding  
will simply disappear into silence.

But there may be  
a deeper movement  
already unfolding within it.

You may notice it  
in the way suffering sometimes softens the heart—  
not immediately,  
not easily,  
but slowly,  
through compassion,  
through patience,  
through a love that remains open  
instead of turning bitter.

You are not asked  
to seek suffering.

Only to carry what comes  
without abandoning love.

And when you do,  
something hidden begins to form.

What wounded you  
does not become your identity.

It becomes a place  
where grace can sing through you.

A tenderness.  
A wisdom.  
A quiet strength  
that could not have been born  
any other way.

And even now,  
the smallest act of faithful love  
is already becoming  
part of a song  
larger than you can yet hear.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Hold me in love  
>
> **Exhale:** Turn my wounds to song
