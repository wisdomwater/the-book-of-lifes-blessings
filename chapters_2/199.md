## 199 - The Garments of Mercy

> *What is given in love becomes radiant*  
> *The smallest mercies are remembered in light*

I saw a vast brightness  
opening like morning across the sky.

Within it moved countless souls,  
quietly luminous,  
as though clothed in light itself.

Their garments flowed like white clouds—  
soft,  
weightless,  
woven through with threads of gold  
that caught the radiance gently.

Nothing about them strained to shine.

They simply reflected  
the beauty they had carried in life.

And a tender wind  
moved among them.

It came softly,  
like breath through a garden at dawn,  
carrying the fragrance of rain, herbs, and flowers.

Where the wind touched them,  
their joy deepened.

And from them rose a sound—  
not loud,  
not distant—  
but full and clear,  
like many streams joining together in peace.

I sensed that every kindness  
had become part of this music.

Every hidden mercy.  
Every burden shared.  
Every quiet act of compassion.

Nothing had been forgotten.

And I understood:

Love offered in ordinary life  
becomes something beautiful  
beyond what the soul can now imagine.

#### Living the Vision

Sometimes daily life  
feels small or unnoticed.

The ordinary rhythms—  
working, helping, listening, caring—  
can seem far removed  
from anything radiant.

And yet,  
you may also notice  
how deeply the heart is shaped  
by simple acts of mercy.

A kindness offered  
when no one sees.  
A patient word.  
A quiet generosity.  
A moment of compassion  
in the middle of an ordinary day.

These things may appear small.

But something within you  
already knows  
they are not empty.

You are not asked  
to become extraordinary.

Only to let love  
move through the life you already have.

Your work,  
your care,  
your attention to others—  
all of it can become  
part of a deeper beauty.

And as you continue  
in these quiet acts of faithfulness,  
something gentle begins to form within you—  
a clarity,  
a softness,  
a radiance  
that does not come from striving,  
but from mercy lived sincerely.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Clothe me in mercy  
>
> **Exhale:** Let my life give light
