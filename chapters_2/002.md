## 2 — The One Who Holds All

> *Nothing is outside the Presence*  
> *All things are gently held*

A vast stillness opened.

Not empty,  
but full—  
like a breath that has no edge.

Within it, a Presence stood.

Not distant.  
Not separate.  
But quietly spanning all things.

Its crown rested in a soft brilliance above the clouds,  
where light does not strain to shine.

Its form descended through a veil of white,  
through the open air,  
through the ground beneath your feet—

and even deeper still,  
into the hidden waters  
where silence gathers.

Yet nothing was divided.

Heaven did not end where earth began.  
The depths were not apart from the sky.

All was one continuous belonging  
within this living form.

Its face was radiant—  
not blinding,  
but too whole to grasp.

And from its breath  
came a gentle sounding—

like wind moving through a hollow reed,  
calling nothing into urgency,  
yet awakening everything.

Three currents moved outward.

One warm with quiet fire—  
alive with beings who rested in shared knowing,  
their awareness like a soft flame  
that neither flickered nor consumed.

Another moved like distant rain—  
a gathering of voices,  
not restless,  
but leaning toward something not yet complete,  
as if listening for a promise  
already stirring beneath them.

The third shimmered with brightness—  
clear and open,  
carrying no strain,  
only a quiet clarity  
that touched whatever it passed.

Nothing clashed.

Nothing hurried.

Each movement belonged.

And the sound that held them all  
was not a command—

but a harmony  
already unfolding.

#### Living the Vision

The world can feel scattered.

As if nothing quite fits together.  
As if meaning slips just beyond reach.

You may notice the impulse  
to gather it all—  
to understand,  
to resolve,  
to make sense of every movement.

But something in you  
already lives within a deeper holding.

Not everything is asking to be solved.

Some things are asking  
to be seen  
from within the wholeness they already belong to.

You may feel it in quiet moments—  
a soft awareness  
that even what feels unfinished  
is not outside the Presence.

That your longing  
is not a sign of absence,  
but of participation.

Something in you listens,  
even now,  
for a harmony that has not yet fully sounded—  
and yet is already here.

You do not have to reach beyond yourself  
to find it.

You are already  
within the One  
who gently holds all things together.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** You hold all things  
>  
> **Exhale:** I rest within You
