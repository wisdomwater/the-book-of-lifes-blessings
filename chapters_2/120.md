## 120 - The Light Already Given

> *The light has never been absent*  
> *Only gently overlooked*

I saw a wide and open field  
resting beneath the morning sky.

Light spread across it—  
not suddenly,  
but steadily,  
touching everything  
without preference.

The earth received it.  
The trees held it in their leaves.  
Even the smallest blade of grass  
seemed to drink it in.

Nothing questioned it.  
Nothing turned it away.

And yet,  
I saw a figure standing there—  
surrounded by light,  
yet searching  
as though something were missing.

It looked far off,  
into the distance,  
as if waiting  
for a clearer sign.

All the while,  
the light rested on its face,  
softly,  
without demand.

Nearby,  
a seed opened beneath the soil.  
Moisture gathered where it was needed.  
Life unfolded quietly,  
without explanation.

And I saw  
that everything was already speaking—  
not in words,  
but in presence.

The light did not insist  
on being noticed.

It simply remained,  
faithful to itself,  
offering itself  
again and again.

#### Living the Vision

You long for something clearer—  
a sign,  
a certainty,  
a voice that removes all doubt.

And when it does not come  
as you expect,  
it can feel  
as though nothing is being given.

But you may begin to notice  
how much is already here.

A quiet pull toward what is good.  
A hesitation before what feels untrue.  
A beauty that stirs something deeper  
without needing to explain why.

These are not small things.

They are gentle ways  
that truth makes itself known.

You do not need  
to force recognition.

Only to pause long enough  
to receive what is already present.

To let the light  
rest on you  
without turning away  
in search of something more certain.

And in that soft attention,  
something begins to shift—  
not outside,  
but within your seeing.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Open my eyes to see  
>
> **Exhale:** You are already here
