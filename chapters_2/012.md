## 12 — The Mystery That Holds

> *Not everything must be known*  
> *All things are already held*

A quiet vastness opened—  
without edge,  
without measure.

Like a sky that could not be counted,  
no matter how long you looked.

Within it,  
countless lights appeared.

Some bright and near.  
Some distant and faint.

Each one distinct.  
Each one known.

Yet no pattern could be fully traced.

No number could contain them.

They did not gather  
to be measured.

They simply were—  
held in a presence  
that saw them all at once.

Nothing was lost in the expanse.

Nothing drifted beyond reach.

Even what seemed hidden  
remained within the same  
gentle awareness.

There was no urgency  
to understand it.

No pressure  
to make it clear.

Only a quiet knowing  
that everything belonged  
to something greater  
than what could be seen.

And in that knowing,  
the vastness did not feel empty—

but full.

Steady.  
Held.  
Complete in its unfolding.

#### Living the Vision

Often, you want to understand.

To make sense of what is happening—  
in your life,  
in others,  
in the unfolding of things you cannot control.

You may notice the questions rising:

Why this?  
Why now?  
What will become of it?

These questions are natural.

They come from a longing  
to feel steady,  
to feel certain.

And yet,  
there are spaces  
where answers do not come.

Not because they are absent—  
but because they are held  
somewhere beyond your current sight.

You may begin to sense  
that you are not meant  
to carry everything into clarity.

Only to remain present  
within what is here.

To trust that what is unseen  
is not uncared for.

That every life,  
every movement,  
every quiet unfolding  
is already known  
in a way you do not need to grasp.

You are not outside the mystery.

You are within it.

And the same presence  
that holds all things  
is holding you.
