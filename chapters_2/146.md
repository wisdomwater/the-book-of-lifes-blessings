## 146 - The Garden Within

> *You are not empty ground*  
> *You are a place where life is forming*

I saw the earth  
resting in quiet fullness—  
dark,  
rich,  
gathering within itself  
what could not yet be seen.

Moisture moved through it,  
softening what was firm.  
Seeds lay hidden,  
held in patience.

Nothing hurried.

And yet,  
beneath the stillness,  
life was already at work—  
unfolding in ways  
no eye could follow.

Then I saw the soul  
like this same earth.

Open.  
Receptive.  
Carrying within it  
a quiet depth.

At first,  
nothing appeared changed.

But slowly,  
something stirred.

A longing rose—  
gentle as breath.

A prayer formed—  
soft as dew.

And where these touched,  
something took root.

Not forced.  
Not shaped by striving.

Simply growing—  
as though it had always known  
how to become.

And I saw  
that this life  
did not come from the soul alone.

It was given—  
received,  
held,  
and allowed  
to rise.

#### Living the Vision

You may feel  
as though little is happening within you.

You may look  
for signs of growth—  
clarity, change, movement—  
and not see them.

But you may also notice  
something quieter.

A desire to return.  
A willingness to remain.  
A small turning  
toward what is good.

These are not small things.

They are seeds.

You do not need  
to make them grow.

Only to keep the ground open—  
to stay present,  
to allow what is given  
to rest within you.

You may not see  
what is forming.

But that does not mean  
nothing is happening.

Life often begins  
in hidden places.

And over time,  
what has been quietly nurtured  
begins to rise—  
not all at once,  
but in its own way.

You are not being asked  
to produce life.

Only to receive it.
