## 20 — The Mystery Beneath the Waters

> *What you cannot see is still held in love*  
> *The unknown rests within a greater knowing*

I saw the presence again—

standing not above,  
but within  
vast and quiet waters.

They stretched beyond sight,  
deep and still,  
like a hidden world  
beneath all things.

There was no fear here.  
Only depth.

A silence  
that did not empty,  
but held.

Within the waters,  
something moved—  
not clearly formed,  
yet alive.

Mysteries rested here,  
unspoken,  
unrevealed.

Not withheld,  
but protected—  
like seeds  
beneath the surface.

And still,  
the presence remained steady.

Not searching.  
Not unsettled.

Resting  
as one who belongs  
within the depths  
as surely  
as within the light.

Nothing was outside this holding.

The heights and the depths  
were not separate places—

they were gathered  
within a single,  
quiet care.

And I sensed  
that even what is unseen  
is not unknown.

Even what is hidden  
is not lost.

All things  
rest within a wisdom  
that does not waver.

And in that resting,  
there was peace.

#### Living the Vision

There are places in your life  
that feel unclear—

questions that remain,  
paths that have not yet formed,  
meanings that stay just out of reach.

You may feel the pull  
to understand,  
to bring light  
into what feels hidden.

And yet,  
there are moments  
when the answers do not come.

When the depth remains.

You may notice  
what rises within you then—  
a tension,  
a reaching,  
a quiet unease.

But beneath that,  
there is something else.

A stillness  
that does not require  
your knowing.

You are not outside  
what you cannot see.

You are already  
within its holding.

And as you begin  
to rest here—  
even gently—  
the need to resolve everything  
softens.

You remain present  
within the mystery,  
not as one lost,  
but as one held.

And in that,  
a deeper trust  
begins to form.

Not in answers,  
but in the One  
who holds them.
