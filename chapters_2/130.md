## 130 - The Open Field of Blessing

> *Another’s good does not close your path*  
> *It reveals how wide the field truly is*

I saw a narrow place—  
enclosed,  
tight,  
as though the air itself  
could not move freely.

Within it,  
everything turned inward.

Heat rose and fell.  
Cold pressed in.  
Nothing settled.

It was a place  
of constant motion  
without rest—  
as though the heart  
could not stop comparing,  
measuring,  
reacting.

Then,  
beyond it,  
I saw something else.

An open field.

Wide,  
quiet,  
unbounded.

The air moved gently there.  
Light rested easily  
on all that grew.

Nothing competed.  
Nothing strained.

Each living thing  
stood in its own place—  
receiving,  
growing,  
untroubled  
by what stood beside it.

And I saw  
that the narrow place  
had no true walls—  
only a turning inward  
that made it feel so small.

The field had always been there.

Waiting.

Unchanged.

As soon as the gaze lifted—  
even slightly—  
space returned.

And with it,  
a quiet freedom  
to breathe again.

#### Living the Vision

Your world can feel small.

A comparison arises—  
and suddenly,  
everything narrows.

Another’s success  
feels close,  
pressing,  
hard to escape.

You may notice  
how quickly the mind begins—  
measuring,  
judging,  
telling quiet stories  
that close the heart.

But there is another movement  
available to you.

A small lifting of the gaze.

A willingness  
to step out of comparison  
and into something wider.

You may try it gently—  
a simple blessing,  
spoken even if it feels unfamiliar:

May their life flourish.

And as you do,  
something begins to open.

The tightness softens.  
The space returns.

You begin to see  
that life is not limited—  
that goodness is not scarce.

There is room for you, too.

You are not outside  
what is being given.

You are already standing  
within it.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Widen my heart in love  
>
> **Exhale:** I bless what is good
