## 112 - The Rain That Blesses

> *What resists can soften into blessing*  
> *Love grows where the heart releases comparison*

I saw a dry and shadowed place  
where the air felt tight—  
as though something  
had closed in on itself.

Nothing grew there.

The ground was still,  
but not at rest—  
held in a quiet tension  
that would not open.

Then,  
from above,  
a gentle rain began to fall.

Not heavy.  
Not sudden.

Soft drops—  
spaced,  
patient—  
touching the ground  
without force.

At first,  
nothing changed.

But slowly,  
the earth began to respond.

The tightness loosened.  
The surface softened.

And where the rain continued,  
something hidden beneath  
began to stir.

Small shoots appeared—  
fragile,  
yet alive.

They did not compete  
with one another.

They simply grew—  
each in its place,  
each receiving what was given.

And the rain continued—  
steady,  
unconcerned  
with where it fell—  
nourishing everything  
without measure.

And I saw  
that this quiet giving  
was stronger  
than the dryness it touched.

#### Living the Vision

You notice the good in others—  
and something within you  
tightens.

A comparison arises.  
A quiet resistance.  
A sense of lack.

You may not choose it.  
But you can feel it.

In those moments,  
there is another movement  
available to you.

You may begin  
with something simple:

a quiet blessing—  
spoken inwardly,  
even if it feels small.

A willingness  
to let the good exist  
without needing to measure it  
against yourself.

This is like the rain—  
gentle,  
steady—  
softening what has become tight.

You do not need  
to force yourself  
into joy.

Only to remain open  
to something larger  
than comparison.

And as you practice this—  
again and again—  
something begins to change.

The heart loosens.  
The ground softens.

You begin to feel  
less divided—  
more able  
to receive and give  
without strain.

You are not diminished  
by another’s light.

You are invited  
to grow in your own.

And quietly,  
you become  
a source of life—  
not by striving,  
but by allowing  
love to move through you.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Let me bless what is good  
>
> **Exhale:** Make me gentle like rain
