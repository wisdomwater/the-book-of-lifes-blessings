## 87 - The Quiet Song Within

> *A deeper music is already sounding*  
> *The soul learns to listen and join*

I saw within the cloud  
a hidden brightness—  
soft,  
yet full.

It did not stand apart.

It rested within—  
like a deeper layer of light  
gently unfolding.

And within that light,  
there was song.

Not loud.  
Not overwhelming.

But clear—  
like a tone  
that carries without effort.

Many voices,  
yet one sound.

Flowing  
like water over stone,  
like wind through open space—  
unbroken,  
whole.

There were forms within it—  
quiet,  
attentive—  
as though listening  
even as they sang.

They were not striving  
to create the music.

They were held within it.

Clothed in a light  
that seemed to rise  
from within them—  
simple,  
unburdened,  
free.

Nothing pulled at them.

Nothing scattered them.

Their attention  
rested in one place—  
and from that stillness,  
the song continued.

It did not begin with them.

It moved through them.

And I saw  
that this music  
had always been present—  
waiting to be heard,  
waiting to be joined.

#### Living the Vision

There are moments when  
life feels full of noise—

thoughts moving quickly,  
attention pulled in many directions,  
a constant sense of motion.

And yet,  
you may also notice  
something quieter beneath it all.

A stillness.  
A subtle awareness.  
A presence that does not rush.

You may feel it  
when you pause—  
even briefly—  
and allow everything else  
to settle.

In that space,  
something begins to emerge.

Not as effort,  
but as resonance.

A sense of alignment.  
A quiet rightness.  
A gentle joy  
that does not depend on circumstance.

You are not creating this.

You are noticing it.

And over time,  
as you return  
again and again  
to this stillness,  
your life begins  
to move differently.

Less driven.  
More attuned.

Like a note  
that finds its place  
within a greater harmony.

You do not need  
to become anything more.

Only to listen.

And allow yourself  
to join  
what is already singing.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Draw me into stillness  
>
> **Exhale:** Let me join Your song
