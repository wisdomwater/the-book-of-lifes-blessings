## 162 - Shaped for Returning Light

> *You are being formed, even now*  
> *Your life is a quiet return to its Source*

I saw a warm glow  
rising in the early dark—  
not harsh,  
but steady,  
like a fire that knows its purpose.

Near it,  
a figure stood—  
not in distress,  
but in stillness,  
as if allowing something unseen  
to work within.

The fire did not consume.

It breathed—  
inward,  
outward—  
gently surrounding,  
gently shaping.

There was no force.

Only a patient presence  
that turned the figure slowly,  
as though every side  
was being brought into the light.

At times,  
the warmth deepened—  
not to harm,  
but to soften  
what had grown rigid.

At times,  
there was only quiet—  
a resting near the fire,  
nothing needing to be done.

And through it all,  
something unseen  
was taking form—  
not added from outside,  
but revealed  
from within.

Above,  
a soft radiance lingered—  
like a home  
already waiting.

And the figure,  
though still in the fire’s care,  
seemed to belong  
to that light—  
as if its journey  
was not toward something unknown,  
but back  
to where it had always  
come from.

#### Living the Vision

You feel the weight. 
of being shaped.

Not always clearly—  
but in the quiet pressures  
of daily life.

A challenge.  
A delay.  
A moment that asks  
for patience,  
or truth,  
or kindness  
you did not plan to give.

It can feel like interruption.

But something deeper  
may be happening.

You may begin to notice  
how these moments  
invite you  
not to become someone else,  
but to become more fully  
what you already are.

There is no need  
to rush this forming.

No need  
to understand every part.

You may simply remain  
near the work—  
present,  
willing,  
open to what is being shaped.

Even now,  
something in you  
is turning toward light.

Even now,  
you are being prepared  
for a deeper belonging.

You do not need  
to reach it all at once.

You are already  
on the way.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Shape me in Your care  
>
> **Exhale:** I return to Your light
