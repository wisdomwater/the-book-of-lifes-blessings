## 108 - The Goodness Within

> *What feels lost is not your origin*  
> *Your beginning is still held in light*

I saw a wide, quiet space  
where the ground sank gently inward.

It felt enclosed—  
not harsh,  
but heavy—  
as though something  
had settled there  
for a long time.

Within it,  
a presence remained low—  
still,  
turned inward—  
holding a quiet sorrow.

Around it,  
the air carried a dimness—  
like a memory  
that had been repeated  
until it felt true.

Then,  
from above and within,  
a soft light began to rise.

Not sudden.  
Not overwhelming.

It touched the space  
without forcing it open—  
like warmth  
finding its way  
into what had been closed.

For a time,  
nothing changed.

Then slowly,  
the presence lifted—  
just enough  
to feel the light.

And in that small turning,  
something deeper was revealed—

not the weight,  
not the sorrow—  
but something beneath it.

A quiet goodness.  
Unbroken.  
Untouched.

It had always been there—  
waiting  
to be remembered.

And the light remained—  
steady,  
patient—  
restoring  
what had never truly been lost.

#### Living the Vision

A quiet thought settles within you—

that this is simply how it is,  
that things will not change,  
that something in you  
is meant to remain this way.

It can feel honest.  
Even certain.

But you may also notice  
a deeper knowing—  
faint,  
yet present.

A sense that this is not  
the whole truth.

You may begin  
by holding both—  
the feeling  
and the possibility  
that something more remains.

You do not need  
to force hope.

Only to leave  
a small opening  
for it.

You may feel it  
in a breath,  
in a pause,  
in a quiet turning  
toward something greater  
than your current understanding.

And in that turning,  
something begins to shift—  
not the outer circumstances,  
but your sense of what is possible.

You are not defined  
by what has happened to you.

There is something within you  
that remains whole.

And gently,  
as you return to that place,  
hope begins to rise again—  
not as certainty,  
but as a quiet trust  
that you are still held  
in goodness.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** I am made in goodness  
>
> **Exhale:** I return to hope
