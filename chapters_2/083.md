## 83 - The Breath of Quiet Light

> *What you are given comes like breath*  
> *Soft, unseen, and full of life*

I saw a stillness  
resting over all things.

Not empty—  
but full,  
like a pause  
before a word is spoken.

Then, gently,  
a breath moved.

It did not rush.

It did not press.

It passed through  
everything—  
like a whisper  
that did not need sound.

Where it touched,  
light awakened.

Not sudden,  
but clear—  
like a glow  
rising from within.

I saw faces lifted,  
not searching,  
but receiving.

Their eyes were open—  
not by effort,  
but by something given.

And within them,  
understanding formed—  
not as thought alone,  
but as something living.

Like a melody  
remembered  
rather than learned.

The breath moved again—  
softly,  
faithfully—  
touching mind and heart  
as one.

Nothing was taken.

Nothing was forced.

Only a quiet giving—  
like life itself  
being offered  
again and again.

And I saw  
that this breath  
had always been near.

Waiting.

#### Living the Vision

Sometimes you try to understand—

to think more clearly,  
to reach for insight  
through effort alone.

And yet,  
you may notice  
how quickly that effort  
becomes heavy.

But there is another way  
that feels more like receiving  
than striving.

You may sense it  
in stillness—  
when you pause  
and allow your thoughts  
to settle.

In that quiet,  
something begins to open.

Not all at once.

Just enough.

A small clarity.  
A gentle knowing.

You may not be able  
to explain it fully.

But it feels true.

You are not creating this.

You are making space for it.

And over time,  
you may find  
that understanding  
comes more like breath—  
arriving,  
filling,  
and moving through you  
without strain.

You are allowed  
to listen this way.

To learn  
through presence.

To trust  
that what is needed  
will be given  
as you remain open.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Breathe Your light into me  
>
> **Exhale:** I receive and rest
