## 121 - The Golden Thread Within

> *Nothing in you stands outside His care*  
> *Even what rises too high is gently bounded*

I saw a figure  
lifting itself upward—  
reaching,  
stretching  
toward a height  
it could not sustain.

There was a brightness about it,  
but it flickered—  
unsteady,  
as though it depended  
on being seen.

It rose for a moment,  
then wavered,  
unable to remain.

And yet,  
within the dimness around it,  
I noticed something else—

a line,  
fine as a thread,  
glowing softly.

It did not strain upward.  
It did not flicker.

It simply remained—  
steady,  
quiet,  
unchanging.

It traced a path  
through the midst of all unrest,  
like a hidden boundary  
that could not be crossed.

The figure moved,  
rose,  
fell,  
shifted in its reaching—

but the thread  
held its place.

And I saw  
that this thread  
was not there to confine,  
but to preserve—

a gentle limit  
that kept everything  
from being lost.

A quiet mercy  
woven even into imbalance,  
holding the way  
back to what is true.

#### Living the Vision

Something in you begins to rise—  
a subtle lifting,  
a quiet sense of being set apart,  
of being more,  
or knowing more.

It can feel almost natural,  
even justified.

But you may also notice  
how quickly it becomes unstable—  
how it depends  
on comparison,  
on recognition,  
on holding a certain image of yourself.

And beneath that movement,  
there is something quieter.

A steady place  
that does not need to rise  
in order to be at peace.

You may feel it  
when you release  
the need to measure yourself—  
when you allow goodness  
to simply be  
what it is.

There is no loss  
in coming back down.

Only a return  
to what is real.

Even when you notice  
that you have drifted,  
that something in you  
has reached too far—

you are not outside the way.

There is still a thread  
holding you.

You can follow it  
with a small turning—  
a breath,  
a soft acknowledgment,  
a quiet return.

And that is enough.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Keep me in Your truth  
>
> **Exhale:** I return to what is real
