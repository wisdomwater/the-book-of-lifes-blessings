## 137 - The Strength That Remains

> *You are not asked to carry everything*  
> *Only to remain where love is calling*

I saw a figure  
drawn inward—  
quiet,  
still,  
as though it had stepped back  
from the movement of life.

Its hands were hidden.  
Its feet were covered.

Nothing reached outward.  
Nothing moved forward.

It seemed safe there—  
untouched,  
unburdened—  
yet also distant,  
as though something within  
had grown dim.

Then,  
beyond it,  
I saw another presence.

Not forceful,  
not loud—  
but steady.

It moved with a quiet strength,  
like roots holding in the earth,  
like a tree standing  
through changing seasons.

It did not avoid the wind.  
It did not flee the weight of weather.

And yet,  
it was not broken.

Its life moved deeper.

What it carried  
did not crush it—  
it shaped it.

And I saw  
that this strength  
was not hardness.

It was a willingness  
to remain—  
to stay present  
even when things were not easy,  
to let life touch it  
without withdrawing.

And in that staying,  
something grew—  
not all at once,  
but steadily,  
like life unfolding  
from within.

#### Living the Vision

You feel the pull  
to withdraw.

To step back  
from what feels demanding,  
uncertain,  
or heavy.

Sometimes,  
this is wisdom.

But sometimes,  
it is a quiet avoidance—  
a desire to remain untouched,  
to keep life at a distance.

You may notice  
where you are holding back—  
where your hands are hidden,  
where your next step  
feels resisted.

You do not need  
to force yourself forward.

Only to ask gently:  
What is the next small place  
I am being invited to remain?

It may not be large.

A conversation you would rather avoid.  
A responsibility waiting quietly.  
A moment of prayer  
when you feel empty.

When you stay,  
even in a small way,  
something begins to shift.

Not everything becomes easy.

But something in you  
becomes steady.

Strength grows  
not by escaping life,  
but by meeting it  
with a quiet yes.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Strengthen me to stay  
>
> **Exhale:** I remain with You
