## 110 - The Quiet Trust

> *You do not need to grasp what holds you*  
> *Light is already reaching toward you*

I saw a vast horizon  
veiled in gentle brightness.

Beyond it,  
something greater remained—  
not hidden in absence,  
but in depth.

The light did not reveal everything.

It shimmered—  
soft,  
steady—  
like a presence  
too wide to be contained.

Below,  
a path stretched forward—  
simple,  
clear enough  
for the next step.

It did not show the end.

Only the way  
just ahead.

And I saw a figure  
standing at its beginning.

It looked toward the horizon—  
not with fear,  
but with quiet wonder.

It did not reach  
to take hold of what it could not see.

It remained.

And in remaining,  
it began to walk—  
not by sight alone,  
but by trust.

With each step,  
the light met it—  
not revealing all,  
but giving enough.

And I saw  
that this was the way—  
not to grasp the horizon,  
but to walk within the light  
that was already given.

#### Living the Vision

You want to understand everything—

to know why,  
to see clearly,  
to feel certain  
before you move forward.

And when that clarity does not come,  
it can feel like something is missing.

But you may also notice  
that not everything is hidden.

There is always something given.

A next step.  
A small clarity.  
A quiet sense  
of what is right.

You can begin there.

You do not need  
to solve the whole path.

Only to trust  
what is already present.

You may feel this trust  
as something simple—  
a willingness  
to move forward  
without needing to control.

And as you do,  
you may find  
that what once felt uncertain  
becomes steady—  
not because everything is known,  
but because you are held  
within something trustworthy.

You are not walking alone.

The light that seems distant  
is already meeting you  
where you are.

And step by step,  
it is enough.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** I trust what is given  
>
> **Exhale:** Lead me in Your light
