## 201 - The Clear Trumpet

> *Words shaped by mercy become light*  
> *True guidance carries the fragrance of love*

I saw within the radiance  
a gathering of luminous souls.

Their garments shimmered deep blue,  
like the stillness of evening sky after rain,  
woven with brightness  
that moved gently as they walked.

Upon their hearts  
were signs that glowed softly—  
living patterns of wisdom and grace,  
held close within them  
like hidden stars.

Their crowns shone warmly,  
clear and steady,  
without pride or display.

And in their hands  
they carried transparent trumpets,  
clear as glass touched by light.

When they lifted them,  
a soft sound moved outward—  
pure,  
beautiful,  
filled with tenderness.

The sound did not overwhelm.

It guided.

And with it came fragrance—  
myrrh,  
sweet herbs,  
the scent of prayer carried through still air.

Those who heard the song  
seemed to breathe more deeply.

Fear softened.  
Weariness eased.

The music gave courage  
to hearts that had forgotten their own voice.

And I understood:

True teaching does not seek to magnify itself.

It becomes clear enough  
for mercy to pass through.

#### Living the Vision

Someone looks to you for guidance.

A child.  
A friend.  
A weary heart searching for direction.

And in those moments,  
it can be tempting  
to speak quickly—  
to offer answers  
before listening deeply.

But there may be  
another way quietly opening.

You may notice it  
in the pause before you speak,  
in the desire to understand  
rather than control.

True guidance  
does not force itself forward.

It listens.  
It discerns.  
It speaks with care.

You are not asked  
to become perfect  
before helping another.

Only to let your words  
remain clear enough  
for love to move through them.

A gentle truth.  
A patient correction.  
A quiet encouragement.

These can become  
like music to another soul.

And when your life itself  
begins to match your words,  
something deeper is heard—  
not only instruction,  
but presence.

A clear trumpet  
through which mercy sounds.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Make my heart clear  
>
> **Exhale:** Let mercy speak through me
