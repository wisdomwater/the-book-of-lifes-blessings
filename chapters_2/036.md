## 36 — The Gentle Fire of Becoming

> *What is not yet whole is still held in love*  
> *And quietly drawn toward fullness*

I heard a sound  
like distant waters—

steady,  
full,  
carrying a quiet longing.

It was not restless.  
It did not strain.

It moved like a deep current—  
sure of where it was going,  
even as it waited.

And within this sound,  
I felt a turning—

not backward,  
but forward—

toward something  
not yet seen,  
yet deeply known.

Then I saw a warmth  
spreading through the expanse—

not sudden,  
not consuming,  
but gentle  
and clear.

Like light  
passing through all things,  
revealing  
without force.

Nothing was hidden.

Not exposed in harshness,  
but made visible  
in truth.

And in this revealing,  
there was no fear—

only a quiet recognition  
of what had been forming  
all along.

Some places  
opened easily  
to the light.

Others softened  
more slowly.

But all were held  
within the same presence—  
a fire  
that did not destroy,  
but completed.

And still,  
there was a sense of waiting—

not emptiness,  
but promise—

as if everything  
was being gently prepared  
for a fullness  
that had always been coming.

#### Living the Vision

At times, you can feel  
that something in you  
is still becoming—

not yet settled,  
not yet whole.

You may notice  
a quiet longing—

not for something outside you,  
but for a deeper coherence  
within.

At times,  
this can feel like waiting.

Like something  
has not yet arrived.

But there is also  
a subtle movement—

a soft illumination  
touching places  
you may not have seen before.

You may feel it  
as a growing clarity,  
a gentle honesty,  
or a quiet invitation  
to let something open.

This is not here  
to rush you.

It is here  
to reveal  
what is already forming.

You are not being forced  
into change.

You are being  
gently shaped  
by a presence  
that knows  
how to bring you  
into fullness.

And even now,  
in the waiting,  
something is already  
coming alive.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Gently shape me  
>
> **Exhale:** Into Your fullness
