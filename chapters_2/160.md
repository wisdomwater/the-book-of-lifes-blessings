## 160 - The Breath Returning

> *You were made to breathe deeply again*  
> *Even the faintest ember can be stirred to life*

I saw a wide, quiet space  
covered in a dim haze—  
not heavy,  
but thin and drifting,  
like air that had forgotten  
how to move.

Nothing stirred there.

The ground was dry,  
holding the shape of what had once grown,  
but no longer reaching upward.

And in that stillness,  
a figure rested—  
not asleep,  
not awake—  
simply unmoving.

The air around it  
carried a faint warmth,  
but no flame.

Then,  
from beyond the haze,  
a soft wind began.

It did not rush in.

It touched the edges first—  
lightly,  
almost unnoticed.

The air shifted.

The dryness loosened.

And where the wind passed,  
something changed—  
not in brightness,  
but in breath.

The figure inhaled—  
slowly,  
as if remembering.

And with that breath,  
a small warmth gathered  
within the chest.

Not fire,  
not yet—  
but a living ember.

The wind did not force it.

It simply remained,  
moving gently through the space,  
until the ember  
held its own quiet glow.

And the air,  
once still,  
began to carry life again.

#### Living the Vision

There are times in life
when everything feels distant.

Not painful.  
Not urgent.

Just…  
quiet in a way  
that feels empty.

You may notice  
how little rises within you—  
no strong desire,  
no clear movement,  
no sense of direction.

And it can be easy  
to remain there.

To let the days pass  
without asking more  
of your own heart.

But something in you  
is not meant to stay  
unmoved.

You may begin  
very gently.

A single pause.  
A breath taken  
with awareness.

Not trying to feel something—  
only allowing yourself  
to notice  
that you are here.

And in that noticing,  
something subtle  
may begin to stir.

A small care.  
A quiet longing.  
A sense that life  
is still near.

You do not need  
to create fire.

Only to welcome  
the breath  
that awakens it.

And to trust  
that even now,  
something in you  
is still alive  
and ready to respond.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Breathe life into me  
>
> **Exhale:** Awaken my heart
