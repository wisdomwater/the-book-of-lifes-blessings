## 205 - The Breath Before Beginning

> *Before all things, Love was already speaking*  
> *The One who formed life still breathes within it*

I heard a voice  
moving softly through the silence.

It did not arrive suddenly.

It came like warm rain upon dry ground—  
gentle,  
steady,  
filled with quiet certainty.

And as it spoke,  
everything seemed to awaken around it.

The air stirred.  
Light deepened.  
The earth itself  
felt held within the sound.

The voice carried no strain.

It was older than time  
and yet nearer than breath.

And I sensed  
that all living things  
had once emerged from this same hidden music—  
waters,  
fire,  
wind,  
the forming of flesh,  
the awakening of the soul.

Nothing stood outside it.

Even what had become broken  
was not abandoned.

I saw a wheel turning slowly in light,  
vast and luminous,  
moving with perfect stillness at its center.

As it turned,  
what had been scattered  
began to gather.

What had hardened  
softened.

What had lost its way  
was quietly drawn home again.

And I understood:

The voice that called creation into being  
still moves through all things—  
restoring gently,  
guiding patiently,  
breathing life  
where life has grown dim.

#### Living the Vision

At times, your life feels scattered.

Thoughts pulling in different directions.  
Desires that conflict.  
A longing for peace  
that seems difficult to reach.

And in those moments,  
it can feel as though  
you must force yourself back together.

But there may be  
a deeper movement  
already at work beneath the surface.

You may notice it  
in small moments of clarity—  
a breath that steadies you,  
a truth that quietly returns,  
a sense that something within you  
still longs for wholeness.

That longing is not separate  
from the One who formed you.

You are not creating healing alone.

You are responding  
to a presence  
already drawing you gently inward.

The wheel is still turning.

Slowly,  
patiently,  
without panic.

You do not need  
to see the whole path.

Only to remain open  
to the next small movement toward life.

A moment of honesty.  
A softened response.  
A return to prayer.  
A simple act of trust.

These are not insignificant.

They are part of the restoring work  
already unfolding within you.

And beneath it all,  
the same breath  
that first gave you life  
continues to carry you home.
