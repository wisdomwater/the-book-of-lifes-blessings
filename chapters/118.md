## 118 - Clothed in Living Praise

> *What moves through you is not yours to hold*  
> *It is given, and gently given back*

I saw a great figure  
standing within the world—  
not apart from it,  
but clothed in it.

The wind moved around Him,  
light and unseen,  
yet full of quiet motion.

The air rested upon Him,  
soft,  
carrying breath  
and subtle life.

The earth beneath Him  
was rich and open—  
green with growth,  
alive with quiet unfolding.

Nothing strained.  
Nothing clung.

All things moved  
in a gentle rhythm—  
coming forth,  
and returning.

The light that touched Him  
did not remain on the surface.  
It passed through—  
as though the brightness  
knew its way home.

And I saw  
that nothing was lost.

What was given  
was not diminished.  
What was shared  
was not taken away.

Everything that flowed outward  
was already held  
within a greater fullness.

And in this,  
there was a deep ease—  
a life unburdened  
by the need to possess  
what was never meant  
to be kept.

#### Living the Vision

Something good rises in you—  
a kindness,  
a clear word,  
a quiet strength to continue.

It can feel personal,  
as though it belongs to you.

And in a sense,  
it does pass through you—  
it takes form in your life,  
your choices,  
your presence.

But you may also notice  
how it arrives.

Gently.  
Unexpectedly.  
Without force.

You did not create it  
from nothing.

Something deeper  
was already moving.

When you recognize this,  
there can be a soft release—  
a loosening of the need  
to hold onto the outcome,  
the recognition,  
the sense of ownership.

You are not diminished  
by letting it pass through.

You are made lighter.

Like breath—  
received,  
and given back.

And in this quiet exchange,  
your life begins to feel  
less like striving  
and more like participation  
in something already whole.
