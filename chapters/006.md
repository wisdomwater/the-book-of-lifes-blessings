## 6 — The Heart That Softens

> *Even what feels closed can open*  
> *Mercy falls like quiet dew*

A dim stillness lingered at the edge of the field.

Not heavy—  
but distant.

Like a presence that had forgotten  
how to move.

A shape hovered there,  
barely formed—  
like mist that had gathered  
without direction.

It did not reach.  
It did not turn.

It only remained.

Watching.

Apart.

The air around it felt untouched,  
as though nothing had passed between  
it and the world.

No giving.  
No receiving.

Only distance.

Then, without sound,  
a softness entered.

Not from above—  
but from everywhere at once.

A fine dew settled  
over the ground,  
resting lightly  
on leaf and stone alike.

Nothing was asked.

Nothing was forced.

The earth did not strive  
to receive it.

And yet,  
it did.

Slowly,  
what had been dry  
began to loosen.

What had been still  
began to soften.

The mist did not vanish—  
but something within it shifted.

A quiet awareness  
of the life it had stood apart from.

And in that awareness,  
the smallest opening appeared—

not an action,  
not a decision—

just a gentle yielding  
to what had always been given.

#### Living the Vision

Sometimes it feels easier  
to step back.

To remain untouched  
by what others carry.

You may notice it  
in subtle ways—  
a turning inward,  
a quiet distance  
from what feels too much.

At first,  
it can feel like rest.

Like a way to protect  
what little energy remains.

And yet,  
something in you  
may begin to feel less alive.

Not broken—  
just dimmed.

There is a different way  
that does not ask you  
to carry everything.

Only to remain open.

You may sense it  
in small invitations—  
a moment to listen,  
a chance to notice,  
a quiet nudge  
toward kindness.

Not overwhelming.  
Not demanding.

Like dew.

It does not force the ground  
to receive it.

It simply arrives.

And something in you  
already knows  
how to welcome it.

You do not have to become  
something new.

Only to soften  
into what has always been  
flowing toward you.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Soften my heart  
>  
> **Exhale:** Let mercy flow
