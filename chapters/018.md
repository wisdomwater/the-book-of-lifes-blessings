## 18 — From Shadow into Full Light

> *What feels unfinished is still becoming*  
> *The light you see is already leading you home*

I saw a soft horizon  
where light was rising—

not sudden,  
but steady.

There were forms in the distance,  
half-seen,  
like shapes held in morning mist.

Nothing was hidden in darkness.  
Only waiting  
to be revealed in time.

As the light grew,  
what had seemed separate  
began to draw together.

Edges softened.  
Distances closed.

It was not that something new appeared—  
but that what was already present  
became clear.

I saw souls  
resting in a quiet peace—

unburdened,  
yet still reaching.

There was no lack in them,  
only a gentle longing—

like eyes  
not yet fully opened  
to the day.

They were whole in their being,  
yet something in them  
awaited a deeper union.

And I sensed  
a promise moving through all things—

that nothing would remain divided.

That what had been known in part  
would be known in fullness.

That the light,  
already present,  
would one day be seen  
without veil or distance.

And in that seeing,  
everything would belong.

Nothing lost.  
Nothing outside.

Only wholeness—  
clear,  
alive,  
and complete.

#### Living the Vision

There are times when  
you feel the edges of your life—

places where clarity fades,  
where understanding feels just out of reach.

You may sense something true,  
but not yet fully see it.

And it can feel  
as though something is missing.

But there is another way  
to meet these moments.

You may notice  
that even in the partial,  
there is a quiet presence—

a light that does not overwhelm,  
but gently guides.

You are not outside of fullness.

You are within its unfolding.

Something in you  
is already whole,  
even as other parts  
are still coming into view.

You are allowed  
to live here—  
in this in-between—  
without needing to rush ahead.

Clarity will come  
in its own time.

And often,  
when it does,  
it feels less like discovery  
and more like recognition.

As if you are seeing clearly  
what has been with you  
all along.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Lead me into fullness  
>
> **Exhale:** I trust what is becoming
