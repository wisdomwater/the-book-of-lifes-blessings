## 18 - The Harmony Within

> *What is received with awareness brings life*  
> *The soul finds balance where it listens deeply*

I saw a quiet room  
filled with soft, golden light.

At its center stood a harp—  
simple,  
beautiful,  
resting in stillness.

No hand moved toward it.  
No sound was forced.

And yet,  
as the light settled upon it,  
a tone began to rise—

gentle,  
clear,  
perfectly held.

Each string vibrated  
not in strain,  
but in response—  
as if touched  
by something unseen.

The sound did not rush.

It unfolded—  
note by note,  
breath by breath—  
filling the space  
without overwhelming it.

Nothing was excessive.

Nothing withheld.

Only a quiet harmony  
that seemed to gather  
all things into balance.

I noticed that the harp  
did not create this on its own.

It received.

It remained open,  
attentive,  
willing to be tuned.

And because of this,  
the music flowed—  
not as effort,  
but as life moving freely  
through what was aligned.

The room itself  
seemed to listen.

And in that listening,  
everything became still—  
as if remembering  
what it means  
to be whole.

#### Living the Vision

There are moments when  
you reach for something—

not out of need,  
but out of restlessness.

It can feel small,  
almost unnoticed—  
a habit,  
a pattern,  
a quiet turning  
toward more.

But you may also notice  
how it leaves you—  
whether it settles you  
or pulls you further  
from yourself.

There is another way  
of receiving.

You may begin  
by slowing down—  
just enough  
to feel what is present.

A breath.  
A pause.

And in that space,  
you may sense  
what is truly needed—  
not what fills quickly,  
but what nourishes deeply.

You do not need  
to deny what is good.

Only to receive it  
with awareness.

To listen for the rhythm  
that brings you back  
into balance.

And as you do,  
something within you  
begins to align—  
quietly,  
steadily—

until your life  
feels less like striving,  
and more like music  
being played through you.
