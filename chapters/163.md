## 163 - The Heart That Rests

> *You do not need to follow every path*  
> *There is a place in you that can remain*

I saw a wide landscape  
filled with many paths—  
some winding,  
some bright,  
some fading into the distance.

Each one seemed to call,  
softly,  
inviting the traveler onward.

And in the midst of them,  
a figure moved—  
pausing,  
turning,  
stepping onto one path,  
then another.

Nothing held.

The ground shifted  
with each step,  
never settling  
beneath the feet.

Then,  
not far away,  
there was a quiet place—  
a small rise in the earth  
where the light rested gently.

No path led to it.

It simply was.

And as the figure slowed,  
drawn not by urgency  
but by something deeper,  
the movement softened.

The turning eased.

One step  
came to rest.

And in that stillness,  
the ground held—  
firm,  
steady,  
unchanging.

The many paths  
did not disappear.

They remained  
at the edges.

But they no longer pulled.

For in the quiet center,  
there was enough—  
a sense of being held  
without needing to search.

And in that resting,  
the heart began  
to listen again—  
not to every voice,  
but to the one  
that had always been near.

#### Living the Vision

There are moments  
when everything feels open—  
many choices,  
many directions,  
many possible ways to go.

And with that openness,  
a subtle pressure may arise—  
a need to choose quickly,  
or to keep moving  
so nothing is missed.

You may notice  
how easily this becomes  
a kind of restlessness—  
a turning from one thing to another  
without ever settling.

Something in you  
longs for another way.

Not less freedom,  
but a deeper grounding.

You may begin  
by allowing yourself  
to stay  
with one small truth—  
something simple,  
something good.

Not forever.  
Just for now.

And as you remain,  
even briefly,  
you may feel  
a quiet stability  
emerging.

A sense  
that you do not need  
to hold every option open  
to be whole.

You are not being asked  
to know everything.

Only to rest  
where you are  
and listen  
for what is true.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Root me in what is true  
>
> **Exhale:** I rest and remain
