## Day 186 - The Swamp of False Power

> *Creation is holy, but it is not God. When the soul seeks power from creatures apart from their Creator, its sight grows blind.*

After this, I saw other evil spirits in the crowd.

They cried out with loud voices:

“Lucifer is lord, because the elements obey his commands.”

These spirits studied the elements carefully and urged human beings toward curses, magic, and sorcery. They persuaded them to search creation for hidden power instead of seeking God.

Then I saw a great swamp.

It seethed as if it were fire and gave off a terrible stench. Within it were many snakes and horrible worms.

There the souls were tormented who, while living in the body, had given themselves to such arts. They had used creatures in rituals of magic and sorcery, trusting the elements more than the living God.

Evil spirits mocked them, saying:

“These are not the true Lord.”

The souls burned in the swamp because they had neglected true faith. They endured the stench because they no longer saw clearly what they were doing. They were tortured by worms because they had placed their confidence in created things rather than in God.

And I saw and understood these things.

Then I heard the voice of the living light:

“The punishments you see are true. Those who wish to overcome the spirits that urge this vice must grieve deeply, humble themselves, and turn away from these deceptions.”

For those who search creatures as though they were Scripture, and serve them as though they were divine, blind the sight of their soul.

Creation should move the heart to praise.

But when the soul questions creation as though it were God, and bends it toward perverse purposes, it harms both body and soul.

Therefore, whoever wishes to draw near the Creator must flee such deception.

#### What This Vision Means

This vision does not reject creation. Hildegard’s world is full of holy signs: waters, winds, stars, trees, animals, and elements all speak of God.

The danger comes when creation is separated from the Creator.

The soul begins seeking control instead of communion. It wants hidden knowledge, secret power, and mastery over forces it was meant to receive with reverence.

The swamp shows the result: what seemed powerful becomes foul, burning, and confused. The snakes and worms reveal spiritual corruption beneath the surface.

True wisdom studies creation as a doorway to praise, not as a tool for domination.

#### Living This Vision

We may not practice sorcery, but we often seek control through other means.

We look for formulas, signs, systems, predictions, or techniques that promise certainty.

But the soul is not healed by control. It is healed by trust.

This vision asks:

Am I seeking God, or only power over my fear?

Let creation lead you back to wonder. Let wonder lead you back to worship.
