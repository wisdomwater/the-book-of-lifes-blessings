## 186 - The Clear Seeing

> *What is received in reverence becomes light*  
> *What returns to the Source is made clear*

I saw a wide landscape  
filled with the beauty of many things—

water moving in quiet streams,  
wind passing through open air,  
light resting gently on the earth.

Each part moved in its own way,  
distinct,  
alive,  
held in a quiet harmony.

Nothing reached beyond itself.  
Nothing claimed to be more.

And at the center of all,  
there was a presence—  
unseen,  
yet unmistakable.

It did not compete with what was made.  
It gave to it.

The water shimmered because of it.  
The wind moved because of it.  
The light revealed because of it.

All things seemed to turn  
softly toward this center—  
not pulled,  
but drawn in quiet recognition.

And as they turned,  
their beauty deepened.

Nothing was taken from them.  
Nothing was diminished.

They became more fully themselves  
by resting  
in what gave them life.

And I understood:

Creation is a doorway—  
not to be held,  
but to be entered with wonder,  
until the heart finds  
the One  
who holds all things.

#### Living the Vision

You notice how life works.

Patterns,  
connections,  
movements that begin to make sense.

And with that noticing,  
you may feel a subtle desire  
to hold it—  
to use it,  
to make it certain,  
to keep it within your grasp.

It can feel like clarity.

And yet,  
you may also notice  
how quickly it becomes tension.

A need to control,  
to predict,  
to stay ahead of what is unknown.

But there may be  
another way quietly present.

You may sense it  
when you pause—  
when you allow what you see  
to remain open.

In that openness,  
something shifts.

What you notice  
no longer feels like something to possess.

It becomes something to receive.

You begin to sense  
that understanding is not the end—  
it is a doorway.

And as you step through it,  
even gently,  
you may feel a quiet return—  
from grasping  
into trust,  
from control  
into wonder.
