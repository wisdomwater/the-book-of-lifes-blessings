## 60 - The Gentle Waiting

> *Even fullness leans toward more*  
> *Love waits with quiet trust*

I saw a soft radiance  
resting in stillness.

It was full—  
peaceful,  
bright—  
like a place where nothing was missing.

And yet,  
within that light,  
there was a turning.

A quiet reaching—  
not restless,  
not strained—  
but open.

Like faces lifted  
toward something  
just beyond sight.

They did not search in confusion.

They waited—  
together,  
held in the same calm certainty.

Their light did not fade.

It deepened.

And within it,  
a gentle longing moved—  
like breath drawn in  
before it is released.

Nothing was hurried.

Nothing was withheld.

Only a sense  
that what was already beautiful  
would become  
more.

Like dawn  
before the sun fully rises.

Like a promise  
already present,  
yet still unfolding.

And I saw  
that this waiting  
was not emptiness.

It was trust—  
steady,  
unshaken—  
resting in what is sure to come.

#### Living the Vision

Something in you  
feels unfinished—

a longing that remains,  
a sense that there is more  
than what you can yet see.

You may feel the pull  
to resolve it quickly—  
to reach for completion  
before its time.

But there is another way  
to hold this space.

You may notice  
that not all longing  
comes from lack.

Some of it  
comes from life itself—  
drawing you forward,  
opening you gently  
to what is still becoming.

You do not need  
to quiet this desire.

Only to let it rest  
in trust.

You are allowed  
to want more—  
more clarity,  
more wholeness,  
more nearness.

And you are also allowed  
to wait.

Not in uncertainty,  
but in a quiet confidence  
that what is unfolding  
will arrive  
in its time.

Something in you  
already knows how to do this.

To remain open,  
without grasping.

To hope,  
without fear.

And in that space,  
waiting itself  
becomes peaceful.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** I wait in trust  
>
> **Exhale:** I rest in what will come
