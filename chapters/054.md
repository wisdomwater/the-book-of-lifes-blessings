## Day 54 – The Fires That Reveal

> *I saw souls passing through fire, each flame revealing what they had loved.*

Then I saw a vast multitude of spirits, fallen and scattered among humanity, each one setting snares according to its nature.

They whispered.

They persuaded.

They stirred longing for what passes away.

And I saw two great fires.

One burned with a pale flame—dim, consuming without intensity. The other burned red—fierce, alive, filled with movement.

Within the red fire were worms.

Some twisted like small serpents. Others had sharp mouths, biting and gnawing. None had legs, yet they moved ceaselessly.

And I saw souls within these flames.

Those who had loved the world lightly were touched by the pale fire. Those who had given themselves fully to it were consumed by the red.

The worms afflicted them—each according to their distortion.

Some suffered from duplicity, having lived divided lives—praising what they secretly rejected, rejecting what they inwardly desired.

Others were tormented for delighting deeply in vanity.

Each soul bore the imprint of its choices.

Each flame revealed what had been loved.

And I understood:

Nothing is hidden.

#### What This Vision Means

The fires are not arbitrary punishment—they are revelation.

They make visible what was formed within the soul during life.

The pale and red flames represent degrees of attachment. The deeper the entanglement, the more intense the purification. The worms symbolize inner distortions—habits of thought, desire, and deception that continue to act upon the soul.

This vision is not meant to inspire fear, but awareness.

What we love shapes us.

What we cling to forms us.

And in the end, those attachments are brought into the light.

Divine justice is not random—it is deeply personal, meeting each soul where it truly is.

#### Living This Vision

This vision asks a quiet but powerful question:

What am I becoming through what I love?

Not in theory—but in practice.

What do you return to, again and again?  
What occupies your thoughts?  
What do you defend, even when it costs you peace?

These are not small things.

They are shaping you.

The invitation is not to live in fear, but in honesty.

To begin aligning your loves with what gives life.

Even small shifts matter.

Because the direction of your love becomes the direction of your soul.
