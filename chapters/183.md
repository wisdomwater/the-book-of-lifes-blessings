## 183 - The Clear Light Within

> *What is seen in truth is gently freed*  
> *What is revealed can return to life*

I saw a quiet light  
standing at the center of all things.

It did not move quickly.  
It did not force its way.

It simply shone—  
steady,  
clear,  
unchanging.

Where it reached,  
nothing was hidden.

Not exposed with harshness,  
but seen  
as it truly was.

Shadows did not resist it.  
They softened.

What had been tangled  
began to loosen—  
not torn apart,  
but gently released  
from what it was not.

The light did not linger  
on what was fading.

It remained with what was real—  
what could live,  
what could grow.

And everything false,  
everything without life,  
quietly fell away—  
like something no longer needed.

There was no struggle in this.

Only a clarity  
that made space  
for what was true.

And I understood:

There is a light  
that searches without wounding—  
that sees fully,  
and restores gently  
what belongs to life.

#### Living the Vision

Something in you  
comes into view.

A pattern,  
a thought,  
a way of being  
that does not feel aligned.

You may feel the urge  
to turn away quickly,  
to cover it,  
to move on.

But there may be  
another way  
quietly present.

You may notice  
a gentle awareness—  
one that does not accuse,  
but does not ignore.

It simply sees.

In that seeing,  
something begins to shift.

Not everything at once.

But the hold it had  
begins to loosen.

You are not asked  
to force change.

Only to remain present  
to what is being revealed.

There is a truth within you  
that does not harm.

It brings clarity  
so that what is not needed  
can fall away.

And what is real  
can begin to rise again—  
not by effort,  
but by being seen  
in the light.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Search me in light  
>
> **Exhale:** Restore what is true
