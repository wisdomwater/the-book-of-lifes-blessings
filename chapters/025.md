## Day 25 - The Trumpet of Obedience

> *All creation responds to the voice of God—each in its own way, yet all in harmony.*

Near the mouth of the great figure  
I saw a white cloud—  
shaped like a trumpet.

From it came a sound—  
clear, full, and alive.

It was not harsh,  
but steady and true.

And I understood:

This was the voice of God.

From His will  
flowed a command—  
gentle, yet irresistible.

It moved through all things.

I saw creation respond.

The rational and the irrational,  
the seen and the unseen—  
all turned in obedience.

Not with reluctance,  
but with readiness.

Each thing honored its Creator  
by being what it was made to be.

For all that comes from God  
is good.

And in its goodness,  
it reflects Him.

The sound of the trumpet  
was not merely a call—  
it was alignment.

A gathering into order.

A harmony  
that held all things together.


#### What This Vision Means

This vision portrays divine order as both authoritative and harmonious. The trumpet represents the expression of God’s will—clear, encompassing, and effective.

All creation responds to this call. Obedience here is not forced submission, but alignment with purpose. Each creature fulfills its role within the greater order.

Human beings, as part of creation, are also called into this harmony. Yet unlike other creatures, they can resist or distort this alignment.

The vision reminds us that goodness is inherent in what God creates. To live in accordance with God’s will is to return to that original goodness.


#### Living This Vision

There is a natural rhythm to life—a way things are meant to flow. Sometimes you feel aligned with it. Other times, you feel out of step.

This vision invites you to listen. Not for loud commands, but for the quiet movement of truth within you. Ask yourself: 

*Where am I resisting what I know is right?*

Obedience is not about losing yourself.  
It is about becoming more fully who you are meant to be. When you align with what is good, you begin to experience a deeper sense of peace. Like a note returning to harmony, your life settles into something steady.

You are part of a larger order—one that is already holding you. All that is asked is that you listen and respond.
