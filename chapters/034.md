## 34 — Restored by Holy Fire

> *Nothing in you is beyond renewal*  
> *What feels dim is being filled with light*

I saw a turning toward the rising light—

a gentle orientation  
toward warmth  
and quiet fullness.

The light did not rush.  
It unfolded—  
like morning  
spreading across the earth.

Everything it touched  
began to awaken.

What had been still  
softened.  
What had been dim  
began to glow again.

There were places  
that felt clouded—  
as though a veil  
had settled over them.

Not broken,  
but hidden.

And the light  
did not turn away.

It entered.

Not sharply,  
but like a warmth  
moving through cold ground.

I saw something like fire—  
not consuming,  
but alive.

It did not destroy  
what it touched.

It filled it.

What had once held only shadow  
now carried a quiet radiance.

Not as it had been before—  
but deeper,  
more open,  
more alive.

And I understood—

nothing was being returned  
to a former state.

Everything was being drawn  
into a fuller light.

What had dimmed  
was not lost.

It was becoming.

#### Living the Vision

Some places in you may feel distant—

like something has faded,  
or slipped out of reach.

You may remember  
a clarity you once had,  
a sense of being aligned,  
and wonder where it went.

And it can feel  
as though something has been lost.

But there are moments  
when a quiet warmth returns—

unexpected,  
gentle,  
without explanation.

You may notice it  
in a softening,  
a new openness,  
a desire to begin again.

This is not a return  
to what was before.

It is something deeper  
finding its way forward.

You are not being asked  
to recover a former version of yourself.

You are being invited  
into a fuller life  
that includes  
everything you have been.

Even what feels like failure  
is not outside this movement.

It, too,  
can be filled with light.

And as you allow this—  
even in small ways—  
you may begin to sense  
that nothing in you  
has been abandoned.

Only gently transformed.
