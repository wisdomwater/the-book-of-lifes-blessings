## 123 - The Hidden Root of Beauty

> *The truest things grow in quiet places*  
> *What is offered in love remains alive*

I saw a figure  
standing in shadow,  
holding something delicate  
in its hands.

A small branch—  
green,  
alive,  
tender with new growth.

It lifted it gently,  
turning it this way and that,  
as though seeking  
the right angle  
for it to be noticed.

On its head  
rested a fragile crown—  
woven of grass,  
fresh for a moment,  
yet already beginning  
to fade.

Then,  
like a breeze passing unnoticed,  
the scene shifted.

The light moved—  
not toward the figure,  
but toward the ground.

There, beneath the surface,  
roots spread quietly—  
hidden,  
unseen,  
drawing life  
from a deeper place.

No display.  
No movement to be admired.

And yet,  
everything above  
depended on this.

The greenness,  
the flowering,  
the quiet strength of growth—

all of it  
rose from what remained  
out of sight.

And I saw  
that what is hidden  
is not lesser.

It is where life  
is made whole.

#### Living the Vision

You do something good—  
and almost immediately,  
something in you turns outward.

A small wondering:  
Did anyone notice?

You may feel  
the desire to be seen,  
to be known  
for what you have done.

There is no need  
to push this away.

Only to recognize it  
with gentleness.

And then,  
to let your attention  
move inward again—  
toward the quiet place  
where the action began.

Would it still be enough  
if it remained unseen?

You may find  
that something in you  
softens at the question.

A deeper intention  
begins to emerge—  
one that is less concerned  
with appearance  
and more rooted  
in love itself.

You are free  
to let some things remain hidden.

To allow goodness  
to return  
to its source  
without holding onto it.

And in that quiet return,  
something becomes steady—  
less fragile,  
less dependent,  
more alive.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Root me in what is true  
>
> **Exhale:** Let love be enough
