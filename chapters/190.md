## 190 - The Gentle Teacher

> *What is seen with love can be guided well*  
> *What is given in wisdom brings life*

I saw a quiet path  
winding through a soft landscape.

Along it walked a guide,  
moving with calm attention—  
neither hurried  
nor distant.

Those who walked beside the guide  
were not all the same.

Some moved with steady steps.  
Some faltered.  
Some paused often,  
unsure of where to place their feet.

And the guide did not treat them alike.

To one,  
a gentle word was given—  
soft,  
encouraging,  
like a hand that steadies without grasping.

To another,  
a firmer voice—  
clear,  
grounded,  
like a staff that keeps the path in view.

Nothing was harsh.  
Nothing was careless.

Each word  
met the one who received it.

And as the journey continued,  
there was no fear in those who walked.

Only a quiet trust—  
that they were seen,  
known,  
and led with care.

The air around them  
was light and clear.

And I understood:

There is a way of guiding  
that does not press or abandon—  
it listens,  
and gives what is needed  
for life to grow.

#### Living the Vision

You respond to the struggles of another.

A word rises within you—  
something to say,  
something to offer,  
something to correct.

It can come quickly.

A desire to help,  
to fix,  
to make things right.

And yet,  
you may also notice  
a quiet pause  
just before you speak.

In that pause,  
something deeper becomes available.

You may begin to sense  
the person before you—  
not just what they have done,  
but where they are,  
what they carry,  
what they may need.

You are not asked  
to have all the answers.

Only to remain present  
long enough to listen.

There is a wisdom  
that does not come from reaction.

It arises  
when you allow yourself  
to see clearly  
and respond  
with care.

And as you do,  
your words may begin to carry  
something different—  
not weight,  
but life.

Not pressure,  
but guidance.

Given in the right moment,  
in the right way,  
they become  
a quiet help  
on the path.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Give me gentle wisdom  
>
> **Exhale:** Let my words give life
