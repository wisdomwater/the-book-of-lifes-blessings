## Day 199 - The White Cloud Garment

> *The works of mercy rise like fragrance before God. Those who live faithfully in the world are clothed in light.*

I saw another radiance, greater and without limit.

Its brightness was so intense that I could not look directly at it, for it was beyond human understanding. The earlier radiance was joined to this one, as one region opens into another.

And I understood that within it were every delight, every music, every voice of praise, joy upon joy, and the magnificence of all rejoicing.

There were souls there who had lived in the world and served God with diligent faith. While still in secular and active life, they observed the commandments of God. They clothed the naked, fed the hungry, gave drink to the thirsty, visited the sick and imprisoned, and showed mercy to every misery.

As in a mirror, I saw some of them.

They wore garments like white clouds, purer than the pure ether above, woven through with gold. Their crowns were like electrum. Their sandals were like glass, reflecting a purity clearer than the clearest waters.

From time to time, a soft wind came from the secret places of divinity. It brushed against them and carried the scent of every herb and every flower.

Then they made the sound of sweet symphony, and their voices rang like many waters.

Their white cloud garments revealed the purity of justice they had practiced. The gold woven through them showed their love for the commandments. Their crowns shone because they had wept over sin with a clear conscience. Their glass-like sandals revealed the upright path they had walked in the world.

And the divine wind touched them because their mercy had become fragrance before God.

#### What This Vision Means

This vision honors faithful life in the world.

Holiness is not limited to withdrawal from ordinary responsibilities. These souls lived in the active life. They served God through mercy, obedience, repentance, generosity, and daily faithfulness.

Their clothing shows that ordinary acts of love become eternal beauty. Feeding, visiting, clothing, comforting—these are not small works in God’s sight. They become fragrance, music, and light.

The soft wind from the divine mystery reveals that mercy carries the scent of heaven.

#### Living This Vision

You do not need to leave the world to become faithful.

Your workplace, home, family, neighborhood, and daily responsibilities can become places of holiness.

The question is not whether your life looks spiritual from the outside.

The question is whether love is being practiced within it.

Who is hungry near you?

Who is lonely?

Who needs mercy?

Every act of compassion is gathered into God’s radiance.
