## 65 - The Inner Light

> *Seeing is more than looking outward*  
> *Light awakens when the heart opens*

I saw a figure  
standing in a dim and shifting space.

Its gaze moved constantly—  
searching,  
turning,  
reaching outward  
in every direction.

It looked closely at everything—  
not missing a detail,  
not resting in one place.

And yet,  
the space around it  
remained unclear—  
as though all this seeing  
could not bring it home.

Then,  
very quietly,  
a different light appeared.

Not outside—  
but within.

Soft at first—  
almost hidden—  
like a glow  
waiting to be noticed.

The figure paused.

For the first time,  
its gaze did not rush outward.

It turned inward—  
just slightly.

And in that small turning,  
the inner light grew clearer—  
not brighter than everything else,  
but steadier.

It did not reveal all things.

It revealed enough.

A path.  
A direction.  
A quiet knowing  
that did not depend  
on constant searching.

And I saw  
that this light  
had always been present—  
not demanding attention,  
but offering itself  
to be received.

#### Living the Vision

You search for clarity—

looking outward,  
gathering information,  
trying to understand  
what is true.

This searching is not wrong.

But sometimes,  
it can leave you restless—  
as though no amount  
of looking  
is enough.

You may notice  
a quieter movement within you.

A sense of knowing  
that does not come  
from effort alone.

It may feel small—  
a gentle trust,  
a quiet intuition,  
a willingness  
to remain open  
even without full certainty.

You can begin there.

You do not need  
to resolve everything.

Only to allow  
this inner light  
to guide you  
step by step.

As you turn toward it,  
something begins to settle.

The urgency fades.  
The need to grasp  
loosens.

You are no longer  
trying to see everything.

You are learning  
to receive what is given.

And in that receiving,  
your vision becomes clearer—  
not because you know more,  
but because you are  
more deeply aligned  
with what is true.
