## 150 - The Clear Turning

> *You do not lose God all at once*  
> *You return by the smallest turning*

I saw a cloud  
gathering before the soul—  
thick,  
shifting,  
layered with shadow and light.

It moved constantly,  
never settling,  
pulling the gaze  
into its motion.

Thoughts rose within it—  
one after another,  
each asking to be followed,  
each offering a path.

And the soul  
stepped forward—  
not with certainty,  
but with habit—  
placing its weight  
upon the moving cloud.

Nothing held.

The ground was unclear.

Then,  
very quietly,  
something deeper  
made itself known.

Not above the cloud,  
but beneath it.

A stillness—  
unchanging,  
unmoved  
by the swirling above.

It did not call out.  
It did not force itself forward.

It simply remained.

And as the soul paused—  
just for a moment—  
its attention shifted.

Not to the many voices,  
but to the quiet beneath them.

The feet drew back  
from the cloud  
and found  
what was steady.

And though the cloud  
did not vanish at once,  
its hold loosened.

The air cleared  
just enough  
for the way to be seen again.

#### Living the Vision

Your thoughts become crowded—  
one leading to another,  
pulling you forward  
into uncertainty.

Plans.  
Questions.  
Concerns.

They can feel important,  
even necessary.

But you may also notice  
how easily they carry you away—  
until you no longer feel  
where you stand.

There is a gentle way back.

Not by solving every thought,  
but by turning your attention  
beneath them.

You may begin  
with something simple—  
a breath,  
a pause,  
a quiet awareness  
of what is present.

You do not need  
to leave your thoughts behind.

Only to stop standing on them.

As you do,  
something steadier  
begins to be felt.

A sense of being held.  
A quiet center  
that does not shift.

From there,  
you can return  
to what is before you—  
not with urgency,  
but with clarity.

You are not lost  
in the cloud.

You are always able  
to turn.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Bring me back to You  
>
> **Exhale:** I rest in what is clear
