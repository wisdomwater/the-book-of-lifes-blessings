## 17 — Held in Hidden Strength
A quiet support beneath all things

> *You are sustained in ways you cannot see*  
> *A tender strength holds you from within*

I saw again the living presence—

not above,  
but surrounding,  
like the very air  
resting against the earth.

There was a softness here.  
A steady warmth.

Nothing strained to remain.  
Nothing forced itself to stand.

All things were held—  
as if resting  
within an unseen embrace.

I sensed a deep support  
beneath the movement of life—

like a quiet strength  
at the center of being.

It did not announce itself.  
It did not draw attention.

Yet everything leaned into it.

There were lives unfolding—  
some marked by weariness,  
some by quiet endurance.

And within them,  
something gentle remained—

a sweetness  
that did not remove the weight,  
but carried it differently.

Not apart from life,  
but within it.

Then I saw  
a subtle joining—

like breath meeting form.

What had once felt separate  
was no longer divided.

The hidden and the visible  
rested together.

Beginning and fulfillment  
were not opposed—

they were one continuous presence.

And within that unity,  
life grew steady.

Not louder.  
Not stronger in appearance.

But deeply rooted—  
held in a strength  
that did not waver.

#### Living the Vision

There are times when  
you feel the effort of holding everything together—

your thoughts,  
your responsibilities,  
your inner world.

You may not even notice  
how much you are carrying  
until the weight begins to show.

And yet,  
there are quiet moments  
when something softens.

A breath deepens.  
A tension releases—  
even slightly.

You may wonder  
where that ease comes from.

It is not something  
you created.

It is something  
you are resting in.

There is a support  
already beneath you—

subtle,  
steady,  
unchanging.

You do not have to reach for it.

Only to notice  
what is already holding you.

And as you begin to trust this,  
even gently,  
you may feel a quiet strength  
emerge within—

not as effort,  
but as participation.

You are not separate  
from what sustains you.

You are within it.

And it is within you.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** You sustain me

> **Exhale:** I rest in You
