# FIRST PART

**THE MAN WATCHES THE EAST AND THE SOUTH**

**BEGIN THE BOOK OF THE REWARDS OF LIFE REVEALED BY THE LIVING LIGHT THROUGH A SIMPLE MORTAL**

These things happened to me, a simple human being, in the ninth year after the Living Light first revealed true visions to me. Those revelations brought me much suffering, which I endured for ten years.

From the beginning, the vision showed me many things. I was taught about the hidden nature of created things. I was given answers and guidance for people—both those of high rank and those of humble life. I also saw a harmony of heavenly revelations. I was shown writings and even a language I had never known before.

After these visions, my body became very weak. A heavy weariness settled over me, and it stayed with me for eight years.

When I turned sixty, another vision came to me—powerful and wonderful. Because of it, I again endured suffering, this time for five years.

Then, in my sixty-first year, in the year of our Lord 1158, when Frederick ruled as Emperor of the Romans and the Apostolic See was troubled, I heard a voice from heaven speaking to me.

The voice said:

“You have received the gift of revelation since childhood—not through the body, but through the Spirit of the Lord. Now share what you see and hear.

“In the beginning, your visions were given like milk to a child—soft and gentle teaching. But later you were shown stronger food—deeper truths meant for those who are ready.

“So now speak—not from yourself, but from Me.
Write—not from yourself, but from Me.”

Just as in earlier visions, there were witnesses: a man and the young woman who cared for me.

At first, I wanted to hide. I wished no one would find me.

But I began to write.

And again the voice from heaven spoke to me, teaching me what I was to reveal.
