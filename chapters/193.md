## Day 193 - The Four Corners

> *At the end of all things, God will reveal the strength that has always held creation. What was stained will be purified, and what was hidden will be brought into light.*

Then I saw the same Man again.

As one who held all things within Himself, He moved at once toward the four corners of the earth.

And upon His left thigh there appeared a Unicorn.

It bent down and licked His knee, and then it spoke:

“The things that were created will be destroyed, and what has not yet been created will be built. The sin of humanity will be examined. The good within the righteous will be perfected, and they will pass into the life to come with a good name.”

I wondered whether another vice would appear, like those I had seen before.

But no such image was shown to me.

Then I heard a voice from heaven:

“The mighty God, whose power is over all things, will reveal His strength at the end of the world, when He transforms it into a new wonder.”

And I understood.

When the Man moves toward the four corners of the earth, it means that God will shake all the ends of the world. Every soul will be prepared to stand before judgment.

The Unicorn shows the Son of God in His holy humanity. He is the One who opposed the devil and brought him down by the sword of purity.

He receives the judgment of the Father.

He proclaims that all things must be purified by fire and renewed in a new way.

The perversity of humanity will be judged.

The holiness of righteous works will be perfected.

Then the souls of the just will pass with great glory and joy into eternal life.

After God perfects the strength of virtue in humanity, He will lift creation into the clouds and remove the ashes that have darkened the elements.

All things stained by human sin will be cleansed.

God will destroy the forces of the North. He will overthrow the devil, strip away his weapons, and take back his stolen spoil.

Then a shining heaven and a pure earth will appear.

What is now dim will shine renewed.

And the blessed human being, purified in body and spirit, will resemble the golden circle of a wheel, burning with life.

All the deepest secrets will be opened.

And the saints will be near to God, filled with joy.

#### What This Vision Means

This vision turns from moral struggle toward final renewal.

The Man moves to the four corners of the earth because nothing will be left untouched. Judgment is universal, but so is restoration. Creation itself will be purified.

The Unicorn is a strange and beautiful image of Christ: purity, strength, and holy judgment joined together. He does not merely condemn evil; He brings creation into its final transformation.

The absence of new vices is important. The old struggle will end. The forces that tempted, divided, and deceived humanity will no longer rise as before.

The goal is not destruction for its own sake. It is a new wonder: heaven shining, earth purified, humanity made radiant.

#### Living This Vision

We often experience life as unfinished.

Our hearts are mixed. Our world is wounded. Even our best efforts feel incomplete.

This vision asks us to hope beyond what we can presently see.

God is not merely patching creation. God is bringing it toward renewal.

The hidden work of purification may feel severe, but its purpose is glory.

Let this hope strengthen you: what is dim will not remain dim forever.

What belongs to God will be made whole.
