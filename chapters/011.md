## 11 — The Faces Within

> *You are not divided*  
> *You are a living harmony*

A wide horizon opened—  
clear and spacious,  
as if the sky itself  
had drawn near.

Within it, four presences emerged.

Not separate—  
but gently woven together,  
like facets of a single light.

One carried a quiet warmth.

Its gaze was soft,  
attentive—  
as though nothing could be seen  
without being felt.

Another stood with steady strength.

Not rigid,  
but grounded—  
like something that could remain  
when the winds began to rise.

A third moved with patient rhythm.

Close to the earth,  
faithful in its offering—  
as if each step  
was already given.

And above them,  
a still awareness hovered.

Wide and clear—  
seeing without strain,  
holding all things  
in a single, unbroken vision.

They did not compete.

They did not pull apart.

Each one belonged to the other.

And together,  
they formed a quiet wholeness—  
a life that did not need to choose  
between tenderness and strength,  
between giving and seeing.

It simply was.

Complete.  
Integrated.  
Alive.

---

#### Living the Vision

There are moments when you feel  
pulled in different directions.

A desire to be gentle—  
and a need to be strong.

A call to give—  
and a longing to understand.

You may wonder  
which one is right.

Which part of you  
should lead.

But something deeper  
is already at work.

You may notice  
how compassion arises  
when you are present.

How strength appears  
when you remain grounded.

How offering flows  
when you trust the moment.

And how insight  
quietly forms  
when you step back  
just enough to see.

These are not separate paths.

They are movements  
within the same life.

Nothing in you  
needs to be left behind.

Only brought into relationship.

You are not asked  
to become one thing.

You are invited  
to become whole.

And in that wholeness,  
something steady begins to emerge—

a way of being  
that does not fracture  
under the weight of life,  
but holds together  
with quiet grace.
