## 97 - The Gentle Fire Within

> *The fire that sees you also loves you*  
> *What is brought into light is already being healed*

I saw a quiet horizon  
just before dawn—  
where darkness thins  
and light begins to breathe.

There,  
a circle of fire  
rested in the air.

It did not rage.  
It did not spread.

It held itself—  
whole,  
complete,  
alive with a steady glow.

Soft flames moved  
along its edge,  
like living breath—  
rising,  
falling,  
never consuming  
what was whole.

At its center,  
the light was clear.

Nothing hidden,  
nothing obscured.

And yet,  
there was no fear  
in its presence—  
only a deep invitation  
to be seen.

A figure stood nearby,  
wrapped in shadow.

Not forced forward.  
Not exposed.

Only waiting—  
as the fire  
quietly remained.

Then,  
with a small turning,  
the figure stepped closer.

Not all at once—  
just enough  
to let the light  
touch what had been hidden.

And where the light met  
what was unseen,  
it did not destroy.

It clarified.

It softened.

It released  
what could not remain  
in truth.

The fire did not grow stronger.

It simply revealed  
what had always been ready  
to be let go.

And what remained  
stood lighter—  
clear,  
open,  
unafraid of the light.

#### Living the Vision

There are places in you  
that prefer not to be seen.

Not because they are beyond love,  
but because they have been  
held in quiet hiding.

You may notice  
how gently you move around them—  
how easily your attention  
turns elsewhere.

And yet,  
something in you  
longs for honesty.

Not harshness.  
Not exposure.

Just a simple willingness  
to let what is there  
be known.

You may begin  
very quietly.

A moment of stillness.  
A breath that lingers.  
A soft acknowledgment  
of what you carry.

Nothing needs to be fixed  
in that moment.

Only seen.

And as it is seen,  
you may sense  
a warmth—  
steady,  
unhurried,  
without judgment.

It does not demand  
that you become different.

It simply makes space  
for what is not true  
to loosen its hold.

You are not being searched  
to be condemned.

You are being met  
so you can be made free.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** See me in Your light  
>
> **Exhale:** Free what is not true
