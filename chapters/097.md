## 97 - The Voice That Awakens

> *A deeper strength rises within you*  
> *It calls you back into life*

I saw a wide horizon  
just before the wind began.

The air was still—  
but full,  
as though something  
was about to speak.

Then a sound moved through the silence.

Not harsh.  
Not breaking.

But deep—  
steady—  
like a voice  
that carries without strain.

It spread across the open space,  
touching everything—  
not to disturb,  
but to awaken.

Where it passed,  
the air grew clear.

What had been dim  
became visible.

What had been scattered  
began to gather.

At the center,  
a presence stood—  
grounded,  
unmoved—  
yet fully alive.

The sound moved through it,  
and from it—  
like breath  
shared without effort.

Nothing resisted.

Nothing was forced.

Only a quiet authority—  
a knowing  
that what is true  
does not need to struggle  
to remain.

And I saw  
that this voice  
did not come to overwhelm—

but to call.

To draw what had drifted  
back into place.

To remind  
what had been forgotten.

And the space itself  
began to feel whole again.

#### Living the Vision

At times, something within you  
needs to become clear—

not gently this time,  
but fully.

You may feel it  
as a quiet firmness—  
a knowing  
that something is no longer right.

It does not come  
from tension.

It comes  
from truth.

You may sense it  
in a decision  
that rises within you—  
to turn away,  
to speak honestly,  
to no longer remain  
in what diminishes you.

This is not force.

It is alignment.

You do not need  
to create this strength.

You may simply  
listen for it.

It often appears  
beneath the noise—  
steady,  
unshaken.

And when you move with it,  
even in small ways,  
something begins to restore itself.

Clarity returns.  
Energy returns.  
Life returns.

You are not resisting the world.

You are remembering  
what is true.

And allowing it  
to shape your next step.
