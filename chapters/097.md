## Day 97 – The Roaring Lion

> *Against the ruin of the soul rises a fierce and living power.*
> *The zeal of God burns not to destroy life, but to restore it.*

Then I saw a great lion standing before these shadows, turned fully against them.

This was the Zeal of God.

It was not like the restless fire of strife, nor the hidden darkness of ruin. Its strength was clear, radiant, and full of purpose. It stood as both majesty and mercy—divine and human joined together.

It roared into the wind with a voice that shook the air.

This roar was not empty. It carried the breath of the Holy Spirit. I saw it flow outward, filling those who were ready to receive it, sending them forth with new life and purpose.

It was a call—a command—to awaken, to speak truth, to reclaim what had been lost.

For the ancient serpent had taken many souls into darkness. But this lion moved with power, breaking the hold of deception, tearing apart the works of the enemy.

No scheme could stand before it.  
No lie could endure its presence.

Even when the forces of darkness resisted, even when they tried to seize what was good, this lion overcame them. It reduced their power to nothing.

Its strength was not chaotic. It was ordered. Directed. Alive with justice.

And I saw that this zeal was not only against evil—it was for life.

It did not roar to destroy the soul, but to call it back.

#### What This Vision Means

The Lion represents the active, powerful love of God that confronts and overcomes evil.

This is not passive goodness. It is not gentle in the sense of being weak. It is fierce, deliberate, and unyielding in its opposition to what destroys life.

The roar symbolizes revelation—the moment when truth breaks through silence and calls the soul to awaken. It is the movement of the Holy Spirit, stirring, empowering, sending.

This zeal works in two ways:
- it destroys deception  
- it restores what has been lost  

It is both judgment and mercy.

The image of God as both divine and human points to a deep mystery: that this power is not distant. It has entered into human experience. It knows our condition and acts within it.

Nothing that opposes truth can ultimately stand before this force.

#### Living This Vision

There are moments when gentleness is not enough.

There are times when something within you must rise and say:  
“This cannot remain.”

Notice where you have tolerated what harms you:
- patterns that pull you downward  
- thoughts that distort truth  
- habits that weaken your spirit  

The zeal of God is not against you—it is for you.

Invite it in.

Let it speak clearly.  
Let it cut through confusion.  
Let it give you the courage to act.

This is not about striving harder.  
It is about aligning with a strength greater than your own.

You do not have to fight alone.

There is a power within you that knows how to overcome.
