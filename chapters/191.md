## 191 - The Hidden Pearl

> *What is honestly offered begins to shine*  
> *What turns toward love becomes beautiful again*

I saw a quiet shoreline  
at the edge of a still sea.

The water moved gently,  
clear enough  
to reveal what rested beneath it.

And there,  
hidden within the deep,  
was a single pearl.

It did not call attention to itself.  
It simply shone—  
softly,  
steadily,  
as if light had found a home within it.

A hand reached down into the water.

Not hurried.  
Not grasping.

The pearl was lifted carefully  
and held close to the heart.

Nothing about it was harsh.  
Nothing carried shame.

It seemed precious  
because it had been found  
through longing  
and honest return.

And as it rested near the heart,  
the whole shoreline brightened.

The water reflected light.  
The air grew clear.

Even the quietness itself  
felt alive with song.

Not loud,  
not distant—  
but near,  
as if all creation  
recognized the beauty  
of something restored.

And I understood:

There is a turning of the soul  
that becomes radiant—  
not because it is flawless,  
but because it has returned  
to love.

#### Living the Vision

You become aware of where you have wandered.

A word spoken carelessly.  
A place within you  
that has closed.  
A way of living  
that no longer feels true.

It can be tempting  
to hide from these things  
or carry them in silence.

But there may be  
another movement  
quietly calling you.

A willingness  
to tell the truth gently.  
To bring what is hidden  
into the light.

You may notice  
that this honesty  
does not push you away from love.

It draws you closer.

Something in you  
begins to soften  
when nothing is concealed.

And in that softening,  
you may feel  
a quiet cleansing—  
not all at once,  
but like water  
moving slowly over stone.

You are not asked  
to become perfect.

Only to return.

Again and again,  
with sincerity,  
with openness,  
with trust that mercy  
is greater than your fear.

And as you do,  
something within you  
begins to shine—  
not for display,  
but because it has come home.

> **Inhale:** Receive my returning  
>
