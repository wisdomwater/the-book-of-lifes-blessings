## Day 200 - The Obedient Song

> *The soul that releases its own will enters a deeper music. Obedience becomes fragrance when it is offered in love.*

In that same great radiance, I saw others as through a mirror.

They wore garments more beautiful than dawn and brighter than the light of the sun. Their clothing was adorned with precious gems and gave off a soft air like the sweetness of flowers, like balm and every fragrant herb.

On their heads were crowns set with noble hyacinth.

Their shoes were wonderfully adorned with precious pearls.

Their voices carried every kind of music, and they sang new songs continuously, never growing weary.

From time to time, a pure and clear light came from the hidden places of divinity and illuminated them. It was so intense and resplendent that no human eye can see it, no human ear can hear it, and no human heart can imagine it.

These were the souls who had submitted themselves to obedience in religious life and served God with full devotion.

They had abandoned their own will through hard work.

They had practiced abstinence from bodily desires.

They had offered others the fragrance of holy virtue.

They praised God with voice, heart, silence, prayer, and good works.

They imitated the angels by humble submission and continuous devotion.

While living in the body, they had separated themselves from the pleasures of the world and turned toward the spiritual life. With true obedience, humble surrender, and full devotion, they served their Creator.

And now they sang without exhaustion in the light of God.

#### What This Vision Means

This vision presents obedience not as oppression, but as sacred offering.

The souls shown here gave up self-will in order to become more fully aligned with God. Their obedience became beauty, fragrance, music, and light.

Their crowns of hyacinth reveal hope. Their pearl-adorned shoes reveal steadiness on the spiritual path. Their endless song reveals a heart trained in praise.

The pure light that touches them comes from the hidden mystery of God. Obedience, when joined to love, opens the soul to divine illumination.

#### Living This Vision

Obedience is difficult for many of us because it can sound like losing ourselves.

But holy obedience is not servile fear.

It is the surrender of the false self so the truer self can live.

Every day gives us small opportunities to release our own will: listening, forgiving, serving, praying, keeping silence, receiving correction.

Where is God asking you to loosen your grip?

There may be music on the other side of surrender.
