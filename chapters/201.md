## Day 201 - Trumpets of True Teaching

> *Those who guide others carry a holy responsibility. True teaching becomes a clear trumpet for those who have no voice before God.*

Again, within the radiance, I saw others as in a mirror.

They wore garments like sapphire, adorned with beryl and pearls. Upon their chests appeared brilliant signs of the seven planets.

On their heads were crowns adorned with topaz, and their shoes shone like pure gold.

In their hands they held trumpets of glass, and from these trumpets came an air scented with myrrh and frankincense. With them they sounded songs and praises for those who had no voice before God.

These were the souls who taught and sustained the people of God.

They had served faithfully through the perfection of good works. In charity, they showed mercy to those entrusted to them. Like the Lord who welcomed sinners and publicans, they guided others toward repentance.

They kept hidden what needed to remain hidden.

They bore the sins confessed to them with holy discretion.

The bright signs on their chest revealed that they had honored the seven gifts of the Holy Spirit. In active life, they cared for the bodily needs of those under their rule. In contemplative life, they sustained them in spirit.

Their crowns showed true justice.

Their golden shoes showed the wisdom of their walk.

Their glass trumpets showed true doctrine—clear, fragrant, and offered for others.

By word and example, they became mirrors.

They led, corrected, taught, protected, and did not allow those entrusted to them to rush toward death.

And through good works, they offered themselves to God.

#### What This Vision Means

This vision honors spiritual teachers, guides, and leaders.

Their beauty comes not from status, but from responsibility carried faithfully. They show mercy. They protect the vulnerable. They teach truth. They keep confidence. They lead by example.

The glass trumpet is a powerful symbol. True teaching must be clear, not clouded by pride. It must carry fragrance, not harshness. It must give voice to those who cannot yet speak before God.

The seven planetary signs point toward the seven gifts of the Spirit, held close to the heart.

#### Living This Vision

Every person who influences others carries a kind of teaching role.

Parents, mentors, pastors, leaders, friends—we shape those who listen to us.

This vision asks us to teach with clarity and mercy.

Do my words help others turn toward life?

Do I keep sacred trust?

Do I lead by example, or only instruction?

A true teacher does not seek admiration. A true teacher becomes a trumpet through which God’s mercy can be heard.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:**  
> Make my words clear

> **Exhale:**  
> Let mercy sound through me
