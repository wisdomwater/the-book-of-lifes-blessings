## 12 — The Light That Reveals

> *Nothing true is hidden from the Light*  
> *What is seen can be made whole*

A quiet brilliance moved through the open expanse.

Not harsh.  
Not sharp.

But clear—  
like morning light  
touching everything it meets.

At the center of that light,  
a Presence stood—  
steady,  
unwavering.

In its hand was something like a blade,  
yet it did not wound.

It shimmered with a living clarity,  
turning gently,  
as though parting what could not be seen.

Where it moved,  
veils grew thin.

Not torn—  
but opened.

What had been hidden  
did not shrink away.

It simply became visible—  
held within the same light  
that revealed it.

Nothing was cast aside.

Nothing was condemned.

Only seen.

Fully.  
Quietly.  
Without distortion.

And in that seeing,  
something softened.

What had been tangled  
began to loosen.

What had been uncertain  
grew still.

The light did not demand change.

It made space  
for truth to appear.

And in that space,  
wholeness began to form—  
not by force,  
but by being known.

#### Living the Vision

You sense something within you  
asking to be seen.

A thought,  
a pattern,  
a quiet knowing  
that lingers just beneath the surface.

You may feel the instinct  
to turn away—  
to keep things as they are,  
undisturbed.

Not out of resistance,  
but out of uncertainty.

What will be revealed  
if you look more closely?

And yet,  
there is a gentler way of seeing.

Not through judgment.  
Not through pressure.

But through a kind of light  
that does not accuse.

You may notice it  
in moments of honesty—  
when you allow yourself  
to simply be aware  
of what is present.

No fixing.  
No defending.

Just seeing.

And in that seeing,  
something begins to shift.

Not because you have forced it—  
but because truth,  
once welcomed,  
naturally opens the way forward.

You are not being measured.

You are being invited  
into clarity.

And clarity  
is a form of kindness  
the soul gives to itself.

> **Inhale:** Let me see clearly
