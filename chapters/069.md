## 69 - The Wings That Hold You

> *Even where light feels distant, you are not alone*  
> *Love stretches wider than the places you fear*

I saw a figure standing  
at the edge of a wide horizon—

where the light softened,  
and the air grew still.

He turned toward the dimmer places,  
not with hesitation,  
but with quiet intention.

From him extended great wings—  
broad,  
gentle,  
alive with presence.

One curved behind him,  
resting like a memory held in peace.

One opened before him,  
welcoming what had not yet come.

And from his shoulders,  
wings stretched outward—  
reaching in both directions,  
holding all things  
in a quiet balance.

They did not beat against the air.

They remained—  
open,  
steady,  
covering what might otherwise feel exposed.

Within them,  
there was a sense of unfolding—

like pages turning  
without sound.

Stories carried,  
not as weight,  
but as wisdom  
held in light.

Above him,  
a soft radiance gathered—

like a cloud  
that shimmered  
with unseen life.

There was movement within it—  
not hurried,  
but continuous—

a gentle rising,  
a quiet procession  
toward something beyond sight.

And though the horizon  
remained open,  
unfinished—

there was no fear in it.

Only a deep knowing  
that nothing was lost.

Everything was being held,  
guided,  
drawn forward  
beneath the wide shelter  
of these wings.

#### Living the Vision

There are times when  
you find yourself  
in uncertain places—

where clarity feels distant,  
and the way ahead  
is not fully seen.

You may feel exposed,  
as if something essential  
is missing.

But you may also notice—  
if you become still enough—  
a quiet sense of being held.

Not removed from the moment,  
but supported within it.

There is a presence  
that does not withdraw  
when things feel dim.

It remains.

You may not always  
understand the path.

You may not yet see  
where it leads.

But something in you  
is still being carried—  
gently,  
steadily,  
without force.

You can rest here.

Not because everything  
is resolved—

but because you are not alone  
in what is unfolding.

And even now,  
something unseen  
is drawing you forward.
