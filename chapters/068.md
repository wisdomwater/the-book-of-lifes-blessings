## 68 - The Clearing of Joy

> *What is true does not need to be forced*  
> *Joy deepens where the heart is clear*

I saw a wide, open meadow  
after a long mist had begun to lift.

The air was soft,  
still holding traces of what had been—  
yet something new was emerging.

Light moved gently across the ground,  
not breaking through,  
but quietly revealing.

Where the fog had lingered,  
it now thinned—  
lifting in slow, almost unseen ways.

And beneath it,  
the earth was alive.

Small flowers opened—  
not bright with display,  
but steady in their presence.

A quiet laughter moved here too—  
but it was different.

It did not echo or scatter.

It rested.

Like a stream moving over stone,  
clear and unhurried,  
carrying nothing that did not belong.

Those who stood in this place  
did not reach for more.

They did not need to fill the silence.

Their joy was not made—  
it was received.

And in that receiving,  
the air cleared further—  
until even the smallest movement  
felt whole.

Nothing was excessive.

Nothing strained.

Only a gentle fullness  
that remained  
without effort.

#### Living the Vision

There are moments when  
you find yourself reaching  
for lightness—

a way to lift the weight,  
to ease what feels heavy.

And sometimes,  
it comes quickly—  
a laugh,  
a distraction,  
a turning away.

But you may notice  
how it leaves you—  
whether it settles you  
or scatters you.

There is another kind of joy  
that arrives more quietly.

It does not rush in.

It waits  
until you are still enough  
to notice.

You may feel it  
in a simple moment—  
a breath,  
a shared glance,  
a word that carries truth.

It does not need  
to prove itself.

It remains.

You are not asked  
to reject joy,  
but to notice  
what kind of joy  
you are receiving.

And as you do,  
something in you  
begins to choose  
what brings clarity  
over what clouds.

This is how the heart  
becomes light again—  
not by striving,  
but by returning  
to what is real.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Clear my heart  
>
> **Exhale:** Restore true joy
