## 92 - The Returning Shape

> *Truth gathers what feels scattered*  
> *Light gently restores what is real*

I saw a wide, dim space  
where nothing held its form.

Edges blurred.  
Shapes shifted.

What seemed solid  
softened and drifted—  
as though it could not remain  
what it was.

There was movement,  
but no direction.

A presence wandered there—  
uncertain,  
unsettled—  
reaching for something  
it could not name.

The air felt heavy—  
not oppressive,  
but unclear—  
like a mist  
that dimmed everything it touched.

Then,  
quietly,  
a light appeared.

Not strong,  
not sudden—  
but steady.

It did not chase the darkness.

It remained.

And where it rested,  
something changed.

Lines began to return.

Edges grew gentle,  
yet clear.

The wandering slowed.

The shifting stilled.

Form emerged—  
not forced,  
but remembered.

What had been scattered  
began to gather.

What had been uncertain  
found quiet ground.

And I saw  
that the light  
did not create something new—

it revealed  
what had always been there,  
waiting  
to be seen again.

#### Living the Vision

At times, things can feel unclear within you—

thoughts that do not settle,  
feelings that seem to shift,  
a sense of being unsure  
of what is true.

It can feel disorienting—  
as though you cannot quite  
find your footing.

But there is a quiet way  
through this.

You may notice  
a simple clarity  
that remains—  
small,  
steady,  
easy to overlook.

A truth you already know.  
A word that feels honest.  
A recognition  
that does not need to be forced.

You can begin there.

Not with everything—  
just with what is real  
right now.

As you rest in that,  
something begins to gather.

Your thoughts soften.  
Your awareness steadies.

You do not need  
to solve everything at once.

Only to return  
to what is true.

And gently,  
your inner world  
begins to take shape again—  
not by effort,  
but by alignment  
with what has always been  
quietly present.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Ground me in what is true  
>
> **Exhale:** I return to the light
