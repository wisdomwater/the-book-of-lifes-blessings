## 195 - The Far Horizon

> *Even in the unknown, grace calls softly*  
> *The heart that turns toward life begins to awaken*

I saw a vast horizon  
covered in deep mist.

It stretched beyond sight—  
quiet,  
hidden,  
filled with distances  
the eye could not cross.

Some places held only shadow,  
soft and dim,  
like a twilight waiting for dawn.

Other places were heavier—  
dense with darkness,  
closed and unreachable.

And yet,  
between the shadows,  
a faint light remained.

Not overwhelming.  
Not forcing itself forward.

Only present—  
steady,  
waiting,  
like a distant lantern  
guiding those willing to turn toward it.

I could not see beyond the deepest darkness.

It remained hidden.

But I sensed  
that the light had not withdrawn.

It stood at the edge of all things,  
calling quietly toward life.

And I understood:

There are mysteries  
the soul cannot fully hold.

Yet even here,  
the invitation remains the same—  
turn toward the light  
while it is near.

#### Living the Vision

There are seasons when life feels uncertain.

Questions rise  
that cannot easily be answered.

You may wonder  
about what lies beyond your understanding—  
about loss,  
about judgment,  
about the hidden places of the soul.

And sometimes,  
the unknown can feel heavy.

But you are not asked  
to carry every mystery.

You are only asked  
to remain open  
to the light you have been given now.

You may notice  
how grace often comes quietly—  
through a longing for goodness,  
through an honest prayer,  
through the simple desire  
to choose life again.

Even the smallest turning matters.

You do not need  
perfect clarity  
to begin.

You do not need  
to understand everything  
to move toward love.

There is already within you  
a quiet awareness  
that knows the difference  
between what closes the heart  
and what opens it.

Stay near that opening.

Let it guide you gently.

And trust  
that every sincere movement toward life  
is already being met  
by mercy.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Draw me toward life  
>
> **Exhale:** Keep me near Your light
