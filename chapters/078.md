## 78 - The Wind of Living Truth

> *The voice of truth does not strive*  
> *It moves like wind and makes all things clear*

I saw an open plain  
beneath a wide and waiting sky.

The air was still at first—  
quiet,  
almost expectant.

Then, from beyond what could be seen,  
a wind began to move.

Not sudden.  
Not violent.

But steady—  
gathering as it came.

It passed over the earth  
with a low, deep sound—  
not harsh,  
but full.

Like a voice  
too large for words.

Where it moved,  
the air became clear.

What had been hidden  
softened and lifted.

What had been tangled  
began to loosen.

Nothing was forced away—  
and yet,  
nothing could remain  
unchanged.

At the center of the plain  
stood a figure—  
still,  
unshaken.

The wind moved around and through—  
not resisted,  
but welcomed.

And I saw  
that this movement  
was not destruction.

It was revealing.

A clearing.

A making-space  
for what was true  
to stand without obstruction.

The wind continued—  
faithful,  
unhurried—  
until the whole plain  
rested in quiet clarity.

#### Living the Vision

At times, something within you  
feels unclear—

when thoughts tangle,  
when old patterns linger,  
when it is hard to see  
what is true.

In those moments,  
you may feel the urge  
to force clarity—  
to push things into order  
by effort alone.

But there is another way  
that comes more quietly.

You may notice  
a gentle movement within—  
like a breath  
that reaches deeper  
than your thoughts.

It does not argue.  
It does not strain.

It simply begins  
to clear space.

You may not see everything at once.

But something softens.

Something loosens.

And what is not needed  
begins to fall away  
on its own.

You are not asked  
to create this movement.

Only to remain open to it.

To stand,  
even in uncertainty,  
and allow truth  
to move through you  
like wind.

And in time,  
you may find  
that what once felt overwhelming  
has been gently  
made clear.

> **Inhale:** Move through me, O Truth  
>
> **Exhale:** Make all things clear
