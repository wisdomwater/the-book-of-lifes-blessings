## Day 86 — The Cloud of the Faithful

> *Those who walk by faith are gathered into a life that does not end.*

And I saw again the white cloud  
surrounding the Man.

It was filled with the souls of the righteous.

And I understood:

From the beginning of creation  
to the coming of the Savior,  
and even now—  
God has been gathering souls.

Some lived before the law.  
Some within the law.  
Some in the grace of baptism.

Yet all who turned toward God  
were drawn into life.

And now they dwell in a place of peace—  
a dwelling prepared from the beginning.

They rejoice.

For they have passed  
from life into life.

They do not turn back.  
They do not fall away.

They move forward—  
like wheels that never reverse,  
carried by the Spirit  
that instructs and purifies.

And I heard a voice  
calling all creation:

“Give praise  
to the One who reigns forever.”

And the souls answered with joy.

I saw that during their earthly lives,  
they had turned away from idols.

They had left behind what was empty.

Some had wandered like strangers  
in unfamiliar lands,  
carrying longing in their hearts.

Yet they followed the commands of God.  
They grew in goodness.

And now—  
they receive what was promised.

Peace.  
Rest.  
Life without end.

#### What This Vision Means

This vision reveals the continuity of salvation across all time.

God’s work is not limited to one era or one group. From the beginning, He has been drawing people toward Himself—through different forms, but toward the same life.

The white cloud represents both purity and unity. These souls are no longer divided by time, culture, or circumstance. They are gathered into one life.

The image of movement—souls advancing and never turning back—speaks of permanence. This is not a temporary state. It is fulfillment.

The mention of those who lived “before,” “within,” and “after” the law emphasizes that salvation is not about a specific system alone. It is about relationship, response, and faith.

This vision offers hope.

The path may differ in its details,  
but its destination is one.

#### Living This Vision

You are part of a much larger story.

Your life is not isolated.  
It is woven into a movement  
that stretches across time.

Others have walked this path before you—  
with doubts, with struggles, with longing.

And they have reached the place of rest.

Let that encourage you.

When the path feels unclear,  
remember that it leads somewhere real.

You do not need to see the end  
to continue walking.

Stay faithful in what is before you.

Let your life slowly turn away  
from what is empty  
and toward what is alive.

And trust this:

Every step taken in sincerity  
is a step toward that cloud of life.
