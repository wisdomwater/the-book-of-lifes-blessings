## 168 - The One Who Holds

> *Nothing falls outside the Living Presence*  
> *What feels unformed is still being carried*

I saw a great figure  
standing in quiet strength.

His gaze moved slowly across the world—  
not missing anything,  
not turning away.

From His feet downward,  
there was a vast deep—  
formless,  
unsettled,  
like waters not yet at rest.

Yet He stood above it  
without strain.

The deep did not rise to claim Him.  
It remained beneath,  
held within His stillness.

And from Him,  
a gentle movement flowed—  
like breath  
passing over water.

Where it touched,  
there was a subtle ordering—  
not forced,  
but shaped from within.

The waters began to quiet.  
What had no form  
began to gather.

All things seemed to rest  
within His presence—  
not contained as something trapped,  
but held  
as something known.

Nothing was outside His reach.

Nothing was beyond His care.

And I understood:

Even what feels unsteady  
is not abandoned.

It is already within  
a greater holding.

#### Living the Vision

There are places in you  
that may feel unclear.

Moments where thoughts shift,  
where emotions rise and fall,  
where nothing seems fully settled.

It can feel like standing  
over something deep—  
something you cannot fully see  
or control.

You may want to step away from it.  
Or try to quiet it quickly.

But there may be another way  
quietly present.

You may notice  
that even here,  
you are not alone within it.

Something in you  
remains steady—  
able to stand  
without being pulled under.

You do not have to resolve everything  
in order to rest.

You do not have to understand  
before you are held.

There is a presence  
already within you  
that does not turn away  
from what feels unformed.

It remains.

It breathes.

It gently shapes  
without force.

And as you become aware of it,  
even for a moment,  
you may feel a quiet trust begin—  
not that everything is finished,  
but that nothing is outside  
the care that holds you.
