## 82 - The Stone of Quiet Seeing

> *What is glimpsed in stillness is enough*  
> *Clarity grows without being forced*

I saw a single stone  
resting within the shelter of a wing.

It was smooth,  
white like marble—  
unbroken,  
steady,  
quietly radiant.

Light touched its surface  
without passing through.

It did not reveal everything.

It reflected just enough.

There were no markings upon it—  
no lines to explain,  
no words to define.

Only a presence—  
firm,  
simple,  
whole.

It seemed to face away,  
as though what it held  
was not yet meant  
to be fully seen.

And yet,  
there was no uncertainty in it.

What was given  
was clear.

What was not given  
remained in peace.

The stone did not reach  
to become more.

It rested  
in what it was.

Held within the wing,  
it belonged  
to something greater—  
a wider unfolding  
not yet complete.

And still,  
it carried truth  
without distortion.

#### Living the Vision

You sense something is true—

not fully formed,  
not completely understood—  
but quietly certain.

You may feel the pull  
to define it,  
to explain it,  
to bring it into clarity  
before its time.

But something in you  
knows how to remain here.

To hold what is given  
without adding to it.

To let understanding  
be simple.

You may notice  
how peaceful this becomes—  
when you allow yourself  
to live within what you see,  
without reaching beyond it.

There is a strength  
in this kind of honesty.

A steadiness  
that does not depend  
on having all the answers.

And over time,  
what once felt partial  
begins to open—  
not because it was forced,  
but because it was trusted.

You are allowed  
to see in part.

You are allowed  
to walk  
with what is enough for now.

And gently,  
clarity finds you  
as you remain  
within the light you have been given.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** I receive what is given  
>
> **Exhale:** I trust what will unfold
