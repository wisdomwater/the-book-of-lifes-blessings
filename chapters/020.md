## Day 20 - The Mystery Beneath the Waters

> *God’s depths are as unknowable as His heights, yet all things remain within His care.*

I looked again,  
and from the calves to the soles of the feet  
the figure stood within the deep waters of the abyss.

These waters were vast—  
dark, hidden,  
full of things unseen.

And I understood:

Just as the heights above are beyond human knowing,  
so too are the depths below.

There are mysteries in God  
that no human mind can reach.

They are like the hidden places of the sea—  
real, yet concealed.

I saw that even the lowest regions  
were not outside His power.

His presence extended into the depths.  
His judgment reached even there.

Nothing was beyond Him.

Yet He did not reveal all things.

Some mysteries remained sealed—  
not out of distance,  
but out of divine wisdom.

For not all knowledge is meant to be held by human understanding.

Then I saw something still and steady.

As a man stands upon his feet,  
so God rests in His works.

He is not troubled.  
He is not uncertain.

Even in the time of great trial—  
when confusion spreads and deception rises—  
those who belong to Him remain steady.

They stand upon what cannot be shaken.

They rest, even in the midst of mystery.

And when the end comes,  
all weakness will pass away.

What remains  
will stand firm forever.


#### What This Vision Means

This vision emphasizes the unknowable depths of God. Just as the heavens are beyond reach, so too are the hidden places beneath. Human understanding has limits, and those limits are not a flaw—they are part of the design.

God’s sovereignty extends everywhere—even into what seems dark or inaccessible. There is no place where His authority does not reach.

At the same time, God chooses not to reveal everything. Mystery is an essential part of divine reality. It invites humility and trust.

The image of standing firmly upon the feet reflects stability. Even in times of uncertainty or spiritual upheaval, those grounded in God remain secure.

In the end, all instability will be resolved. What is now hidden will find its place within the greater order of God’s purposes.


#### Living This Vision

There are questions you carry that do not have answers.

Why certain things happen.  
What lies beyond this life.  
How everything fits together.

It can be unsettling to live with these unknowns. But this vision offers a quiet reassurance: You are not meant to understand everything. Some things are held in depths you cannot reach.

And that is okay.

Instead of striving to uncover every mystery, you can learn to stand within them. Ask yourself:

*Can I trust what I cannot see?*

Trust does not remove questions.  
But it gives you a place to stand. Even now, in the midst of uncertainty,  
you are held within a reality that is stable and sure. And one day, what feels hidden will no longer trouble you.

You will stand in fullness—with nothing left to fear.
