## 80 - The Word Within

> *What was written at a distance draws near*  
> *and begins to breathe within you*

I saw a wide stone path  
stretching across a quiet landscape.

Its surface was marked—  
lines and forms  
carved with care,  
clear,  
unchanging.

And yet,  
no one stood upon it.

It waited.

Then, slowly,  
light began to gather—  
not above,  
but within.

It moved like warmth  
beneath the stone,  
softening what had once been rigid.

And from the light,  
a presence emerged—  
not separate from the path,  
but living through it.

Where there had been only form,  
there was now movement.

Where there had been distance,  
there was nearness.

The markings did not disappear.

They became alive—  
no longer something to follow from afar,  
but something carried  
from within.

The path was no longer outside.

It unfolded  
wherever the presence moved.

And I saw  
that this nearness  
did not demand.

It invited.

Not to strive,  
but to walk  
in quiet union  
with what was already living.

#### Living the Vision

There are moments when  
you find yourself doing  
what is right—

yet something feels  
distant.

As if your actions  
are reaching outward,  
but not quite touching  
what is alive within you.

You may notice  
a gentle longing  
for something more than correctness—  
something closer,  
more real.

And then,  
perhaps quietly,  
you begin to sense  
that what you seek  
is not far.

There is a presence  
within you  
that already knows  
how to move in truth.

It does not push you  
from the outside.

It draws you  
from within.

You may feel it  
in small moments—  
when an action arises  
without strain,  
when kindness flows  
without effort,  
when you respond  
from a place of quiet trust.

This is not something  
you have to create.

It is something  
you are learning  
to notice.

To follow.

To live from.

And gently,  
what once felt like obligation  
begins to feel like life.
