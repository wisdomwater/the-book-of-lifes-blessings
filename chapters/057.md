## 57 — The Gentle Turning Within

> *You are always being invited to turn*  
> *Even now, you can move toward what is true*

I saw a quiet path  
opening through a field of light—

simple,  
unmarked,  
yet unmistakably clear.

It did not demand  
to be followed.

It simply remained—  
present,  
waiting,  
available.

And I saw a figure  
standing nearby—

not bound,  
not lost,  
but paused.

As though something  
had been noticed.

There was no urgency,  
no force—  
only a quiet awareness  
of direction.

The path did not change.

The light did not shift.

But something within  
the one who stood there  
began to soften—  
to listen,  
to feel,  
to turn.

And with that turning,  
the first step came—

small,  
unsteady,  
yet true.

Nothing was taken away.  
Nothing was demanded.

But with each step,  
there was a growing alignment—  
a sense of movement  
that matched something deeper.

The path did not rush.

It met each step  
as it came.

And in this meeting,  
there was a quiet restoration—  
a life slowly  
coming back into harmony.

#### Living the Vision

There are moments when  
you become aware  
of a quiet misalignment—

a way of being  
that no longer feels true,  
a pattern that no longer  
brings life.

You may feel  
a hesitation—  
a wondering  
if you are ready  
to respond.

But the turning  
does not begin  
with certainty.

It begins  
with willingness.

You may notice  
a small opening—  
a desire to move  
in a different direction,  
even if you do not  
yet know how.

That is enough.

You do not need  
to change everything.

You can take  
one honest step.

And then another.

There is a wisdom  
that meets you  
along the way—  
not outside of you,  
but also not limited  
to what you already know.

It guides gently.

It clarifies slowly.

And as you follow,  
something in you  
begins to align—  
not through effort alone,  
but through presence  
and trust.
