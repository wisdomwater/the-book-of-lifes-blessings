## 8 — The Song of Alignment

> *You are always being gently called*  
> *To return to what you already are*

I saw a soft brightness  
near the place of breath—

like a cloud  
shaped by sound.

From it came a tone—

clear,  
steady,  
unhurried.

It did not startle.  
It did not demand.

It simply moved—  
like a note  
carried on open air.

And as it moved,  
everything listened.

The waters shifted  
into their quiet rhythm.

The trees seemed to lean  
into their own stillness.

Even the unseen currents  
found their place.

Nothing strained to respond.

Nothing resisted.

Each thing  
became more fully  
what it already was.

The sound did not command.

It revealed.

It gathered all things  
into a gentle harmony—  
where nothing was out of place,  
and nothing was left behind.

And I sensed  
that this sound  
was always present—

not distant,  
but near—

like a quiet song  
beneath every moment,  
waiting to be heard.

#### Living the Vision

There are moments when  
something in you feels out of rhythm—

when your thoughts,  
your actions,  
your inner sense  
do not quite align.

You may try  
to correct this  
through effort—

to force yourself  
into clarity.

But there is another way  
already unfolding.

You may begin to notice  
a quieter guidance—

not loud,  
not insistent,  
but steady.

A sense of what feels true.  
A pull toward what feels right.

It may be subtle—  
easy to overlook.

But when you pause  
and listen,  
it becomes clearer.

You are not being asked  
to become something else.

You are being invited  
to return  
to what is already within you.

And as you respond—  
even gently—  
you may feel  
a soft settling.

Like a note  
finding its place  
within a song.

There is no rush.

Only a listening  
that leads  
to belonging.
