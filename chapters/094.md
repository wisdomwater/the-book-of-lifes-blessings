## 94 - The Warmth That Comes Near

> *You do not need to become strong first*  
> *Love comes close and makes you whole*

I saw a great light  
rising gently—  
not distant,  
but near enough  
to be felt.

It moved across the horizon  
like morning,  
steady,  
unhurried,  
touching everything  
in its path.

Nothing called it down.

It came on its own—  
a quiet giving  
of warmth and light.

And where it touched,  
what had grown cold  
began to soften.

What had been hidden  
was gently revealed—  
not exposed in harshness,  
but held in a tender brightness.

I saw it enter  
the smallest places—  
the low ground,  
the fragile edges,  
the places easily overlooked.

Nothing was beneath it.

Nothing was passed by.

And I understood  
that this light  
did not wait  
for readiness.

It came  
because it was its nature  
to come.

And in its nearness,  
everything changed—  
not all at once,  
but enough  
for life to begin again.

#### Living the Vision

There are moments when  
you feel weak—  
not only in body,  
but in heart.

Uncertain.  
Unsteady.  
Not as strong  
as you wish you were.

You may feel  
as though you must fix this first—  
become better,  
clearer,  
more capable  
before anything can change.

But this vision  
offers another way.

You do not need  
to prepare yourself  
for what is already coming.

You may notice  
a quiet warmth  
reaching you  
even now—  
in small ways,  
in gentle moments,  
in a sense of being met  
where you are.

You do not need  
to hold it together.

Only to allow  
what is near  
to touch you.

Your weakness  
is not a barrier.

It is a place  
where something real  
can enter.

And as you receive it—  
even slightly—  
something begins to shift.

Not by effort,  
but by nearness.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Draw near to me  
>
> **Exhale:** Warm what is weak
