## 35 — The Countless Living Light

> *What is alive in love does not fade*  
> *It widens beyond what can be counted*

Within the wide expanse,  
I saw a gathering—

not distant,  
not fading,  
but quietly radiant.

They were not separate  
from the light.

It moved through them  
like breath—  
clear,  
unobstructed.

There was no strain  
in their being.

Only a fullness—  
as if life itself  
had opened  
and continued.

They could not be counted.

Not because they were hidden,  
but because they were  
too many to hold  
within a single gaze.

And yet,  
each one  
felt known.

Together,  
they formed  
a gentle unity—

not forced,  
not blended away,  
but harmonized—

like many streams  
becoming one water.

Their presence  
was like a quiet song—

deep,  
steady,  
unbroken.

And within it,  
there was a sense  
of wholeness still unfolding—

a soft remembering  
of all that had been lived,  
held now  
without loss.

Nothing felt ended.

Only carried.

Only becoming.

#### Living the Vision

Life can feel fragile—

as if what you are living  
might slip away  
or be forgotten.

You may wonder  
what remains  
of what you have given,  
what you have loved.

But something in you  
knows  
that what is real  
does not vanish.

You may feel it  
in quiet ways—

a memory that still warms,  
a connection  
that does not fade,  
a sense that love  
has a way of continuing  
beyond what you can trace.

This is not something  
you have to prove.

It is something  
you are already part of.

You are not moving  
toward disappearance.

You are moving  
within a life  
that keeps unfolding—

held,  
known,  
and gently widening.

Even now,  
you belong  
to something  
that cannot be counted.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** I am held in life  
>
> **Exhale:** Love does not end
