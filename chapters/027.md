## 27 — The Winds of Justice

> *You are quietly being drawn toward what is true*  
> *Nothing offered in love is ever lost*

I saw a living current  
moving like wind through light—

not restless,  
but steady,  
as if it knew its way  
without searching.

It remained near—  
close to the place of seeing,  
like a presence  
that does not turn away.

There was a warmth within it,  
a quiet brightness—

not consuming,  
but clear.

It did not rush forward.  
It did not pull.

It simply held its course,  
faithful to something deeper  
than motion itself.

Around it,  
other currents moved—

soft,  
layered,  
like breath unfolding.

They carried a sense of time—  
of something long and continuous,  
gently weaving what had been  
with what was now.

Nothing felt separate.

Everything belonged  
to a single, quiet flow.

And within this movement,  
I sensed a nearness—

like a word  
resting within breath.

Not spoken from afar,  
but formed  
from within.

It did not interrupt.

It accompanied.

And I understood—  
nothing offered in love  
had disappeared.

All of it remained,  
held within this gentle current,  
drawn toward a fullness  
not yet seen,  
but already present.

#### Living the Vision

Sometimes you wonder if what you do matters—

the small choices,  
the quiet kindness,  
the unseen effort.

They can feel  
as though they pass unnoticed.

But there is something  
holding them.

You may begin to notice  
a quiet reassurance—

not in words,  
but in a sense  
that what is true  
remains.

You are not alone  
in your movement.

There is a guidance  
that stays near—

not forcing,  
not correcting,  
but gently drawing.

You may sense it  
in a pause,  
in a soft turning  
toward what feels right.

It does not demand certainty.

Only a willingness  
to respond.

And as you do—  
even in small ways—  
something within you  
begins to align.

Not all at once.

But slowly,  
steadily,  
like breath  
finding its rhythm.

You are being led  
from within.

And nothing given in love  
is ever lost.
