## 28 — One Flame, One Will

> *You are not asked to disappear*  
> *You are gently drawn into wholeness*

I looked into a living brightness—

and saw a multitude  
beyond number.

Each one radiant,  
distinct,  
fully alive.

Yet nothing was scattered.

No movement clashed.  
No light dimmed another.

They moved together—  
like many flames  
sharing a single fire.

There was no effort  
to become one.

No striving  
to remain apart.

Each simply rested  
in the same desire—  
a quiet turning  
toward what is true.

And in that turning,  
they were gathered.

Difference remained—  
clear,  
beautiful,  
unhidden.

Yet it did not divide.

It belonged.

I sensed a deeper stillness  
beneath their movement—

like a quiet knowing  
not yet spoken.

Something held  
just beneath the surface—  
not concealed,  
but waiting.

Like a soft stirring  
before the wind arrives.

Nothing was rushed.

Nothing was forced into light  
before its time.

And within it all,  
there was peace—

a trust  
that what was unseen  
would unfold  
when it was ready.

#### Living the Vision

There are times when  
you feel pulled in different directions—

as if parts of you  
are not yet in agreement.

You may try  
to silence one voice,  
or elevate another.

But something in you  
remains unsettled.

What if the invitation  
is not to remove  
what feels divided—

but to bring it  
into a deeper center?

You may begin to notice  
what feels most true,  
most life-giving,  
most aligned with love.

And as you gently turn  
toward that,  
something begins to gather.

Not all at once.

But slowly,  
your inner world  
starts to feel less fragmented.

What once competed  
begins to soften.

You are not being reduced.

You are being brought together.

And for what remains unclear—  
what has not yet come into view—  
you do not need to force it.

Some things unfold  
in their own time.

Your part  
is simply to remain present—  
to listen,  
to trust,  
to allow.
