## 9 — The Joy That Endures

> *Not every sweetness satisfies*  
> *A deeper longing lives within you*

A warmth moved gently across the land.

Light touched the surface of things—  
skin,  
air,  
the quiet spaces in between.

There was a sweetness there,  
immediate and inviting.

A figure stood within it,  
open to every sensation—  
hands reaching,  
gathering what could be held,  
what could be felt,  
what could be tasted in the moment.

Nothing hidden.  
Nothing restrained.

Only a steady turning  
toward what pleased.

And for a time,  
it was enough.

The warmth.  
The nearness.  
The fullness of now.

But beneath it,  
something quieter stirred.

Not in opposition—  
but beneath the surface.

Like a deeper current  
moving under still water.

The light shifted.

Not fading—  
but widening.

And the sweetness that once lived only at the edges  
began to open inward—

revealing a depth  
that could not be grasped  
by the hands alone.

The reaching softened.

The breath slowed.

And what had once been sought outwardly  
began to rise from within—

a quiet joy,  
unbound by moment or form,  
steady as a hidden spring.

Always present.  
Always giving.

#### Living the Vision

There are moments when life feels most alive  
through what you can feel.

A taste,  
a touch,  
a sound that draws you in.

You may notice how naturally you move toward these things—  
how they brighten the day,  
how they bring a sense of closeness to life.

There is goodness here.

And yet,  
you may also sense  
that something in you  
is not fully satisfied.

Not lacking—  
but searching.

A deeper longing  
that no single moment can hold.

It does not rush.

It does not demand.

It simply remains—  
quietly inviting you  
to notice it.

You may feel it  
when everything becomes still,  
when the outer movement slows  
just enough  
for something within  
to be felt.

This longing is not a problem to solve.

It is a doorway.

A gentle turning  
toward a joy  
that does not depend  
on what comes and goes.

You are not being asked  
to leave behind the beauty of life.

Only to discover  
the deeper life  
from which all beauty flows.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Awaken what is deeper  
>  
> **Exhale:** Let lasting joy rise
