## Day 196 - The Empty Vessel

> *Evil has no true life of its own. It begins in refusal, feeds on deception, and collapses into the emptiness it chose.*

What more can mortals learn about the danger of forgetting God, except that what is rejected before Him falls among endless torments?

The old serpent does not desire the good.

He inspires evil and all the works of sin.

He was the first to behold the radiance of God, and yet he began the evil that should never have existed. Every creature was made by God, but the evil that began in the ancient serpent began apart from Him.

Lucifer had been made like a mirror, adorned with brightness. But he wanted to be light in himself, not a reflection of the Light.

Then God set the sun in the heavens to flood all creatures with radiance against Lucifer’s false glow. He set the moon to illumine darkness against his traps. He set the stars to obscure and overcome the vices.

God is fullness. In Him there is no empty place.

But the devil is an empty vessel.

As soon as he saw himself and became proud, he lost his radiance and was buried in hell. There he remains without glory and without praise.

He is the predator who drove the first human being from paradise.

He is the thief who stole innocence.

He is the murderer who killed Abel.

He is the deceiver who attacked humanity by pretending to be God.

The devil’s evil is worse than human evil, for humanity did not see God as He is. The devil did, and turned away.

Therefore he remains in torment, an exile without consolation.

His deception twists back upon himself. He burns with rage when those who followed him are taken from him through repentance and purification.

And I understood that evil has no lasting kingdom.

It is emptiness pretending to be power.

#### What This Vision Means

This vision names evil as a rejection of reflected light.

Lucifer was created radiant, but not as his own source. His fall began when he wanted to be light apart from God. This is the root of all deception: the creature refusing to receive.

God, by contrast, is fullness. The sun, moon, and stars become signs of divine generosity, light given against darkness.

The devil is called an empty vessel because evil cannot create true life. It can only distort, steal, and accuse. It feeds on what God has made.

Yet even this darkness is frustrated whenever a soul repents. Mercy robs the thief of his stolen goods.

#### Living This Vision

There are times when false light looks convincing.

Pride can feel like strength. Control can feel like wisdom. Self-protection can feel like safety.

But anything cut off from God eventually reveals its emptiness.

This vision invites us to ask:

Am I trying to be light, or receive light?

The soul becomes whole not by shining from itself, but by reflecting the One who is fullness.

Return to the Light that does not empty you.
