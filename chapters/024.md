## 24 — The Unsearchable Light

> *You are touched by what you cannot contain*  
> *The light you meet is greater than your knowing*

I lifted my gaze  
and saw a living brightness—

not distant,  
but near enough  
to be felt.

It did not press forward.  
It did not demand to be seen.

Yet it shone  
with a fullness  
that could not be held.

Like fire,  
but gentle.

Like light,  
but alive.

There was no edge to it.  
No boundary to follow.

Only a radiance  
that extended beyond sight—  
as if what was visible  
was only the smallest part.

And still,  
it remained present.

Touching all things  
without being contained by them.

I sensed a quiet movement  
within the light—

like currents  
flowing without end.

Each one  
a mystery.

Each one  
complete,  
yet opening into more.

Nothing was exhausted.  
Nothing repeated.

And yet,  
all belonged  
to a single presence—

vast,  
unsearchable,  
and deeply near.

I could not take it in.

But I could remain  
within it.

And that was enough.

#### Living the Vision

There are moments  
when you come close  
to something you cannot explain—

a sense of wonder,  
a quiet awe,  
a feeling of depth  
that your thoughts cannot reach.

You may try  
to understand it,  
to give it form.

But it slips beyond  
your grasp.

And something in you  
may feel the tension of that—  
the desire to hold  
what cannot be contained.

Yet there is another way  
to be here.

You may notice  
that even without understanding,  
you are still touched.

Still held  
within something real.

You do not need  
to define what you encounter  
to receive it.

You are allowed  
to rest in the experience—  
to let it remain open,  
alive,  
unresolved.

And in that openness,  
something deepens.

Not knowledge,  
but presence.

Not certainty,  
but connection.

You are not losing anything  
by letting mystery remain.

You are making space  
for a deeper seeing—  
one that does not grasp,  
but gently receives.
