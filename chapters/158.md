## Day 158 - Glory Due His Name

> *When the soul returns to its Creator, even the devil is put to shame. A human life becomes praise again.*

Whenever a person recognizes their sins and leaves them behind, God is made known in the soul.

The soul begins to yearn for God.
The soul begins to see God.

And when that person starts to build just and holy works, the angelic order is honored. When the fragrance of good deeds spreads among others, the cherubim write the secrets of God.

The devil is ashamed when a human being abandons sin and returns to the Creator.

For the devil hardened himself in wickedness and would not return. But the faithful soul turns back and gives glory to God for everything that belongs to salvation.

So the Psalmist cries:

“Give to the Lord glory and honor. Give to the Lord the glory due His name. Worship the Lord in His holy courts.”

And I understood:

You who desire to avoid evil and fulfill what is good, give the Ruler of the world all glory with true faith. Serve justice. Become doers of holy works.

Give glory to the Lord when you call Him God and truly believe He is God.

Give glory when you do good works for Him, remembering that you are made in His image and likeness.

Worship the Lord with soul and body.

Bow inwardly and outwardly. Honor Him with abstinence, chastity, and every virtue that walks in His ways.

Imitate the harmony of the celestial and angelic order.

The faithful soul does these things while still in the body. It escapes the suggestions of the devil. It clings to its Creator. It quickly turns away from whatever tries to suffocate it.

Whoever desires life should receive these words.

And keep them deep in the heart.


#### What This Vision Means

This vision shows repentance becoming praise.

The soul does not return to God merely to avoid punishment. It returns so that life may become rightly ordered again.

When a person leaves sin and begins holy works, creation rejoices. The angelic order is honored because the human soul has resumed its place in divine harmony.

The image of the devil being ashamed is powerful. Evil cannot bear the sight of return. It wants the soul to believe that failure is final. But repentance proves otherwise.

The Psalm calls the soul into worship—not only with words, but with embodied life.

To give God glory is to live truthfully, justly, and humbly.

Praise is not confined to song.

A healed life becomes worship.


#### Living This Vision

You glorify God when you return.

Not only when you feel strong.
Not only when you succeed publicly.
Not only when your life looks holy from the outside.

You glorify God when you turn from what suffocates you and cling again to life.

One honest confession can be praise.
One repaired relationship can be praise.
One act of restraint can be praise.
One humble beginning can be praise.

Do not despise small obedience.

The angels understand what it means.

Every return matters.

Every true turning puts despair to shame.


#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:**  
> Let my life praise You  

> **Exhale:**  
> Keep me close to life
