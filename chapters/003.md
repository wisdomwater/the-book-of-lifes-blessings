### THE FIRST IMAGE

The first image looked like a human being, but its skin was dark like that of a person from Ethiopia. It stood naked, with its arms and legs wrapped tightly around a great tree. The tree had many branches and was covered with every kind of flower.

The figure gathered the flowers and held them in its hands.

#### 1. WORDS OF WORLD LOVE

“Every kingdom of the world belongs to me, along with all its beauty and ornaments. Why should I fade away when I can hold such vitality in my hands?

Why should I grow old when I could remain in the freshness of youth? Why should my bright sight become dim and blind? That would bring me shame.

So I will enjoy the beauty of this world as long as I can. I will embrace it with delight.

As for the other life—I do not know it. And the stories people tell about it mean nothing to me.”

But as soon as the figure finished speaking, the great tree dried up from its roots. It withered completely and fell into the darkness I had seen before—and the figure fell with it.

#### 2. RESPONSE OF DIVINE LOVE

Then I heard a voice from the stormy cloud answering the image:

“You act with great foolishness if you think a full life can be found in a spark that quickly turns to ash.

You are not searching for true life—the life where youth never fades and old age never wears the soul down.

You live in darkness, surrounded by black fog. Like a worm, you hide within human desire. Your life lasts only a moment. You dry up like grass, and then you fall into the lake of ruin along with everything you gather around you—those things you call flowers.

But I am a pillar of heavenly harmony. I give the joys of true life.

I do not reject life itself, but I crush what harms it. That is why I stand against you.

I am the mirror of all virtues. In me, every faithful soul may see itself clearly. But you wander through the paths of darkness, and the work of your hands leads only to rebellion.”

---

### THE SECOND IMAGE

The second image looked like a hunting dog. It stood upright on its hind legs. Its forehead leaned against a straight staff, and it wagged its tail happily.

Then it spoke.

#### 3. WORDS OF PETULANCE

“What could possibly stop a person from laughing and enjoying life—even if the humor is a little crude?

The breath of the soul is beautiful and musical. What kind of person would live with nothing but thoughts of death? No one.

So let us enjoy ourselves while we can.”

#### 4. DISCIPLINE RESPONSE

Again I heard a voice from the stormy cloud answering the figure:

“You are full of mischief and mockery. You laugh at everything and drift like the wind, blown in every direction. Your restless behavior is like worms that dig endlessly through the earth.

When people see you, they are easily drawn to you. Like a friendly dog, you run toward them with cheerful playfulness, persuading them to follow their own desires.

But your words are empty and harmful. They wound the heart.

You turn your whims into law and trap people inside them.

But I wear the belt of holiness and the cloak of integrity. I stand beside the dignity of true order, like an honored companion at a royal marriage.

Through me, discipline shines like a noble lineage adorned with justice.”

---

### THE THIRD IMAGE

The third image also looked like a man, but his nose was twisted. His hands were like the claws of a bear, and his feet were like those of a bird of prey. His hair was black, and he wore pale clothing.

Then he spoke.

#### 5. WORDS OF VAIN AMUSEMENT

“It is better to enjoy life than to be sad. Pleasure is not a crime.

Everyone who knows God rejoices and sings. Heaven itself delights in creation. Why should I not be happy too?

If I only brought sorrow to people, they would run away from me. So instead I will create many kinds of amusement so that everyone may rejoice with me.

God made the air that carries sweet sounds to my ears. He gave flowers that delight my eyes.

Why should I not enjoy them?

Even people find pleasure in animals, and animals find pleasure in people. That is simply the way things are.”

#### 6. MODESTY’S RESPONSE

From the stormy cloud I heard another voice reply:

“You worship your own desires like an idol. Your sound is empty, like a hollow noise made by human hands.

You carry both human and animal impulses, and your behavior reflects them both.

Your actions belong to mortal creatures—not to the living, but to the dying. You chase whatever you want, wandering along the restless paths of vanity.

But I turn away from such things.

I shelter myself beneath the wings of the cherubim. I learn the mysteries of God through wisdom and sacred teaching. I am filled with the life of heaven.

I look with innocent eyes and seek honesty in my conduct according to the will of God.

But you flee from the will of God, wandering in the blindness of your ignorance.”

### THE FOURTH IMAGE

The fourth image had the shape of a man, but its body looked like thick smoke. It had no human limbs—only two large black eyes.

It did not rise or move from place to place. Instead, it remained still in the darkness I had seen before.

Then it spoke.

#### 7. WORDS OF HARDNESS OF HEART

“I did not create anything, and I did not bring anyone into existence. So why should I work and wear myself out?

I will not labor for anyone unless it benefits me.

If God created everything, then let Him take care of it.

If I am friendly with someone and ask about their troubles, what do I gain from it?

I will do nothing—neither good nor bad.

And if I spend all my time worrying about others, what peace would I have? What kind of life would that be?

No. I will take care of myself, and let everyone else take care of themselves.”

#### 8. RESPONSE OF MERCY

Then again I heard a voice from the stormy cloud replying:

“O heart of stone! What are you saying?

The flowering plants give their fragrance. Even the stone releases moisture. All creation offers something of itself.

Every creature serves humanity in some way—and does so willingly.

But you are hardly worthy of the human form you wear. From you comes only a cruel gaze, empty of mercy. You are like bitter smoke, darkened by evil.

But I am like a living plant, filled with gentle life that grows in air and dew. My heart is full, so I can pour help into the lives of others.

I was present when the command first went forth that all creation should serve humanity. But you were not part of that harmony.

My eyes see every need, and I make it my own. I help the discouraged find courage again. I am like balm for pain, and my words bring healing.

But you—you are only bitter smoke.”

---

### THE FIFTH IMAGE

The fifth image had something like a human head, except that its left ear was like a hare’s ear—so large it covered its entire head.

The rest of its body was like a boneless worm curled inside a hole, like a child wrapped tightly in cloth.

In a harsh voice it spoke.

#### 9. WORDS OF LAZINESS

“I do not want to offend anyone, because I might lose my comfort or the help they could give me.

If I upset someone, I could ruin my own life and lose my friends.

So I will honor the rich and powerful. But I will not care about the poor or the holy—because they cannot benefit me.

I will simply be pleasant with everyone so that I do not suffer trouble.

If I strike someone, they might strike back harder. If I do someone a little harm, they might repay me with greater harm.

So while I live among people, I want peace.

Whether people do good or evil, I will stay silent.

Sometimes it is better to lie and deceive than to tell the truth. It is better to gain something than to lose it. It is better to flee from strength than to fight against it.

Why begin something you cannot finish?

Even if the brave and wise mock me, they only receive what fate gives them. I choose the safety of my own home.

Those who speak truth often lose their possessions. And those who fight sometimes lose their lives.”

#### 10. RESPONSE OF DIVINE VICTORY

Then I heard a voice from the same stormy cloud answering:

“Your first error was raising your voice against God and refusing to follow justice.

So you wandered in your terrible dullness, turning away from what is right. Whenever opportunity appeared, you deceived others.

You have no integrity.

But I hold the sword of God’s strongest virtues, and with it I destroy injustice.

I will strike you with that sword.

You are nothing but ashes of ashes. Everything you gather will become small and empty.

I refuse to live in the ashes and empty lies of the world. Instead, I seek the fountain of living water.

I fight against the ancient serpent. With the wisdom of God’s scriptures I tear down all its defenses.

And so I will always dwell with the true God.”

---

### THE SIXTH IMAGE

The sixth image had the face of a man, but its mouth was like a scorpion’s. Its eyes were distorted, with whites larger than the pupils.

Its arms looked human, but its hands were twisted and ended in long claws. Its chest, belly, and back were like a crab. Its legs resembled those of a lobster, and its feet were like vipers.

It was tangled inside a mill wheel, gripping the upper spokes with its hands and the lower spokes with its feet.

It had no hair and wore no clothing.

From its mouth it threw out words like burning sparks.

#### 11. WORDS OF ANGER

“I crush and trample anyone who offends me.

Why should I endure an insult?

If someone does not want harm from me, then they should not harm me first.

I will strike with my sword. I will beat with my staff anyone who dares insult me.”

#### 12. RESPONSE OF PATIENCE

Again the voice from the stormy cloud spoke:

“My voice is heard in the heights, and it reaches down to the earth like healing balm.

You are a deceiver who thirsts for blood, always coming from the cold north.

But I am the gentle breath of life that brings forth flowers and the fruits of virtue. I build steadily in the hearts of people, finishing what I begin because I remain constant.

I trample no one. I remain calm, and no one condemns me.

But if you build a tower of rage, I will bring it down with a single word. I will strip it apart and scatter it.

You will pass away.

But I will remain forever.”

---

### THE SEVENTH IMAGE

The seventh image looked human from the head down to the back, but its hands were like those of a monkey.

From the waist down it looked like a goat. Its feet were hidden in the darkness, so I could not see them clearly.

It wore no clothing and stood completely naked.

Then it spoke.

#### 13. WORDS OF NEARLY JOY

“I live a sweet and pleasant life. Why should I deny myself?

God gave me this life. Why should I not satisfy the desires of my body?

Copper begins dark and hard, but when polished it shines like gold. So if there is some impurity in my flesh, it is not my fault.

Many people live blindly without understanding what they do.

But I know this life, and I want to live it fully.”

#### 14. RESPONSE OF DESIRE FOR GOD

Again the voice from the stormy cloud answered:

“You stand naked, yet you feel no shame for living a blind and speechless life instead of seeking the life where no darkness remains.

Your actions break justice and truth. You do not live as a rational being.

But I know that the life of this world withers like grass. So my desire reaches toward what never fades.

Within me lives the harmony of heaven—the joy of angels and spirits.

I have made an alliance with that joy, and I will never abandon it.”

---

Then I saw that the great Man I had first seen was holding a sword with three sharp edges. He lifted it and swung it from side to side.

And the sword itself spoke.

#### 15. WORDS OF THE SWORD

“I burn with zeal against the North and against all who dwell there.

Who can defeat me? Who can bring me down?

No one.

I am not made of earthly matter. No man or woman brought me into existence.

But I judge every human work.

God formed humanity from the clay of the earth. And I examine their deeds just as a person looks into a mirror.”

---

### 16. THE INFIDELS ARE NOT KNOWN AND THE WORKS OF GOD REMAIN IN FORCE

Then I heard a voice from heaven saying:

“Who can count the works of God? Who can number the beings who live in His kingdom?

Who are they? What are they like?

Many speak with one another while spreading the darkness of division. They search for their god in the north and worship him according to their own corrupt desires.

In doing so they twist and destroy the good things God has created.

They judge in their hearts, saying, ‘This life is good, that life is bad.’

They imagine they know more than God—but they do not even understand what they are doing.

Yet the works God performs in humanity endure forever.

As the prophet Ezekiel shows when he writes about the four living creatures.”

### 17. EZEKIEL’S WORDS

“As for the form of their faces: each had a human face. The four also had the face of a lion on the right, the face of an ox on the left, and the face of an eagle.” (Ezekiel 1:10)

This vision carries a deeper meaning.

The **human face** shows that humanity is the work of God alone. It points to God’s compassion, which gives the human heart the ability to feel and to care.

The **face of the lion** represents the strength and wisdom of God. Through this strength, human beings receive reason and courage. It appears on the right side because the mercy and power of God lift up the good works inspired by Him.

The **face of the ox** points to sacrifice. It reminds us that God Himself became the offering and teaches humanity how to give itself back to God. It stands on the left because sacrifices can be imperfect—some are accepted, and others are not.

In the same way, when a person offers their will to God, they may still be tied to earthly concerns while turning their thoughts toward heaven. Yet God draws that person closer through the act of offering. Then He gives grace so that the person may truly give themselves to Him.

The **face of the eagle** represents the knowledge of God. It shows the wisdom that allows a person to recognize God and understand His will.

This wisdom rises above the others. It is powerful and awe-inspiring, and for humanity it is life itself. That life never fades. It moves everywhere, seeing everywhere. In the human soul it shines like stars in the sky.

---

### 18. MAN CANNOT CALCULATE WHO SHALL DWELL IN HEAVEN AND WHO, SEDUCED, SHALL DWELL WITH THE DEVIL

Human beings may gain much knowledge, yet no one can count the works of God or know how many will inherit the kingdom of heaven.

Just as the wonders of God cannot be numbered, so the number of those who reach the heavenly dwellings through good works cannot be counted.

In the same way, the number of those who are led astray by the devil’s temptations and end up with him is also beyond counting.

But God knows the number of them all.

---

### 19. THERE ARE MANY SECRETS IN GOD THAT HE DOES NOT REVEAL

There are many mysteries within God. Most of them He reveals only in part, and only when it pleases Him.

God alone knows everything. He holds all things within Himself and sustains everything He has made. All things are preserved according to His wisdom and grace.

This vision itself bears witness to that truth.

The great **Man** I saw—whose height reached to the clouds of heaven and whose feet stood in the depths of the abyss—represents God.

---

### 20. WHY GOD CAN BE CALLED “MAN”

This figure is called “Man” because through Him all things exist, and from Him all things come.

He is the Man of whom the prophet speaks:

### 21. WORDS OF THE PROPHET ISAIAH

“The Lord goes forth like a hero. Like a warrior He stirs up His strength. He raises a shout and cries aloud, and shows Himself mighty against His enemies.”
*(Isaiah 42:13)*

This means the following.

Before time began, the Lord brought all creatures into existence through His strength and power. The life He created carried within it the seed that would allow all living things to grow and multiply.

Everything He made was good, created according to the plan He had formed from the beginning. He also placed within creation the structure of the virtues, so that nothing necessary would be lacking.

But there were enemies who rose up against Him in pride. Before even seeing the ladder, they tried to climb it. Before finding a seat, they tried to sit upon it. Their works were empty illusions. And just as illusions fade away, so pride leads only to destruction.

The Man who rises in strength and anger represents the burning fire of the angelic hosts. When the enemy tried to darken heaven with evil, these heavenly choirs drove him out.

In the joy of victory, and by the powerful will of God, the angels cried out with one voice:

**“Who is like God?”**

Their cry roared like a mighty storm. All the armies of heaven proclaimed that the enemy had fallen and that war would no longer exist in heaven.

So the ancient enemy fell into darkness, stripped of light and the joy of life. Yet even then he prepared his weapons, filling his quiver with arrows to fight against the will of that great Hero.

But the same Man triumphed over his enemy once again through another work—through a work that resembled Himself: the human being whom He created.

Through humanity, God began again to fight against the arrows and cunning schemes of the ancient enemy.

---

### 22. GOD WILL FIGHT AGAIN WITH THE OLD ENEMY AND WILL DESTROY HIM

Then the **Word made flesh** lifted His banner, and the battle began again.

This struggle will continue until the full number of His brothers and sisters—the saints—is complete.

With great strength, this Man will again fight the enemy, just as He once fought him in heaven. And in the end He will destroy him completely.

For the enemy builds his power on sin, deception, and wickedness.

But those who do not share in these things are blessed. Their happiness will never end.

---

### 23. HUMANITY CAN SPEAK ONLY OF THE TIME BETWEEN BIRTH AND DEATH

The greatness of this Man stretches from the beginning of creation to the end of the ages.

But human beings can only understand the small portion of time between their birth and their death.

They do not know what came before them. They do not know what will come after them.

Only God exists without beginning and without end.

---

### 24. ONLY GOD KNOWS WHAT EXISTED BEFORE THE WORLD

The shoulders of this great Man rise above the clouds into the clear and peaceful sky.

This means that before the world began, God alone knew all things within the hidden brightness of His divine being.

God stands above all things and beyond all things. Even angels and the souls of the righteous cannot fully grasp His greatness.

All creatures come from God. But God Himself has no beginning and depends on nothing.

He lives in Himself.
He has power in Himself.
He knows through Himself.

God is the One who **lives**, the One who **has power**, and the One who **knows**.

Through these three powers, all His works are perfectly formed.

And because they come from Him, everything He has made carries within it the possibility of reaching the fullness for which it was created.

### 25. WHY ETERNITY IS FIRE, AND THIS FIRE IS GOD

God is eternal. Eternity is like fire—and this fire is God.

But it is not a hidden fire, nor a quiet flame. It is a living and active fire.

The power of God is far beyond the understanding of any creature. He orders and governs everything through the brightness of His mysteries, just as the head directs the whole body.

In this way God creates rational life:
eyes that see,
ears that hear,
noses that smell,
and a mouth through which reason speaks.

God is the head of all believers.

Yet He does not reveal everything hidden in the mystery of His divinity. Within Him remain the deepest secrets of life.

Scripture says that by His will He makes His ministers “flames of fire” (Hebrews 1:7). For every decision is first conceived before it is carried out. So it is with God.

Before the time of the Law—represented by the shoulders of the great Man—God tested humanity through fire and water. At that time there was no written law by which people could be judged.

Later, through the Law, He purified people with strong discipline and correction.

And finally He completed this work when He Himself became human and destroyed the power of the devil.

In His mercy He cleansed humanity from its sins through true righteousness. As the prophet says:

---

### 26. WORDS OF THE PROPHET ISAIAH

“Authority rests upon His shoulder.”
*(Isaiah 9:6)*

This means that justice appeared in the world through a man who carried the power of God.

When God became human, He destroyed the works of the devil through the strength of His power and broke the gates of hell.

Through His apostles He brought baptism to the whole world, restoring justice to humanity—humanity that had been formed from the dust of the earth but had become mortal.

In the beginning, when humanity was like the first tender bud of a flower, the devil deceived it. The enemy opened humanity’s inner life and poured out corruption, spreading the poisonous seed of desire throughout the children of humankind.

But God formed a body in the purity of the Virgin through the warmth of the Holy Spirit. This body was not born from that poisoned seed.

Therefore, when God became human, He cleansed humanity from the poison of the devil.

And this sinless Man saved sinners by washing away their sins.

---

### 27. FROM THE ORIGIN OF CREATION UNTIL CHRIST SOME MYSTERIES WERE SHOWN, AND OTHERS REMAINED HIDDEN

From the shoulders down to the thighs, the great Man stood beneath the clouds, within another white cloud.

This shows that from the beginning of creation until the time when truth appeared on the earth, God worked many miracles in the hidden majesty of His divinity.

Some things He revealed openly through miracles.

Other things He kept hidden—mysteries that remain bright and alive within the secret life of the Spirit, guarding and guiding holy souls.

These mysteries cannot be fully explained to the human mind.

---

### 28. THE OLD LAW WAS THE SOUND OF THE WORD, BUT NOT THE WORD ITSELF

From the shoulders downward, God established a chosen people.

He gave them the commands of the ancient Law. Yet that Law was like the **sound of a word**, not the Word itself.

When someone speaks, the sound of the word reaches the ear before the meaning is fully understood.

In the same way, the ancient Law was the **echo** or **shadow** of the Word that was to come.

The true Word appeared later—Christ.

---

### 29. AS THE BODY IS SUPPORTED BY THE KNEES, SO GOD SUSTAINS CREATION

From the thighs down to the knees, the Man stood in the air that surrounds the earth.

Just as human life flows from the thighs and the body is supported by the knees that allow it to move, so God sustains all creation.

He nourishes life through the warmth and moisture that support the living body.

In the same way, a new people was born through true purity. Though they still suffer on earth, they receive comfort and are refreshed by the sweetness of miracles and virtues.

When the divine power joined with the Virgin through the breath of the Holy Spirit, the Word became visible in human form.

Through this event the new law was revealed.

Then the **sound** and the **Word** became one, as the old and new laws were brought into harmony.

---

### 30. THE OLD LAW WAS SHADOWED, BUT THE NEW LAW IS CLEAR

The ancient Law was dim and shadowed. The new Law stands in the open light.

In a similar way, the souls of the blessed who are now in heaven do not yet possess their full joy, because they are still without their bodies.

They do not yet see the face of the Father in complete fullness.

Something that is divided cannot see the whole.

But when their bodies are restored, they will behold God fully and perfectly.

Then, just as the new Law reveals clearly what the old Law only hinted at, their vision will become complete—and it will never fade or change again.
