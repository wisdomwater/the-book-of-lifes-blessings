## 37 — The Light That Holds

> *What is true remains, even when unseen*  
> *What is held in light cannot be undone*

I saw a field of quiet radiance—  
wide,  
steady,  
untroubled at its center.

A gentle movement passed through it—  
like wind  
that knows its path.

It did not rush.  
It did not force.

It carried a clarity—  
a presence  
that felt whole.

And wherever it moved,  
something awakened—

not suddenly,  
but like a slow unfolding  
toward what had always been.

Then, at the edges,  
a heaviness gathered.

Not sharp,  
but dense—

like a shadow  
trying to settle.

It pressed inward,  
seeking space,  
seeking to cover.

For a moment,  
there was tension—

a meeting  
of what was clear  
and what was not.

But the light did not retreat.

It did not struggle.

It remained.

And the shadow,  
though present,  
could go no further.

It thinned  
at the boundary—

held  
by something deeper  
than resistance.

Within the light,  
a warmth began to glow—

like a hidden sun  
rising from within.

And beside it,  
a softer light—  
gentle,  
reflective,  
carrying that same brightness  
into the surrounding space.

Nothing was hurried.

Nothing was lost.

Only a quiet assurance—  
that what is rooted in light  
cannot be overcome.

#### Living the Vision

There are moments when  
you feel the weight  
of what presses in—

uncertainty,  
discouragement,  
a sense that something  
is trying to dim  
what you know is true.

You may feel it  
at the edges of your awareness—  
a heaviness  
that asks you to turn away  
from what is steady.

But something in you  
remains.

Even when your clarity fades,  
even when you feel unsure,  
there is a quiet center  
that does not move.

You may notice it  
in small ways—

a returning breath,  
a flicker of trust,  
a sense that you are still held  
even here.

You do not need  
to push the darkness away.

You only need  
to stay  
with what is real.

And when your own light  
feels faint,  
you may find  
you can still reflect—

carrying a gentleness,  
a kindness,  
a presence  
that echoes something deeper.

This is enough.

The light  
does not leave you.

It holds.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Hold me in Your light  
>
> **Exhale:** I remain in You
