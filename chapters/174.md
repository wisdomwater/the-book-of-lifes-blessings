## 174 - The Clearing Waters

> *What is not true fades away*  
> *What is living is gently renewed*

I saw a wide landscape  
after a long stillness.

The ground held many traces—  
marks and remnants  
left by what had passed through.

Some were heavy,  
pressed deep into the earth.

Others were faint,  
already beginning to disappear.

Then,  
from a quiet height,  
water began to flow.

Not in a rush,  
not with force—  
but steady,  
clear,  
unfolding across the land.

It moved into every hollow,  
every unseen place.

Where it passed,  
the ground softened.

What had been hardened  
began to loosen.

What did not belong  
was gently lifted,  
carried away  
without struggle.

And beneath it,  
something fresh appeared—  
clean earth,  
ready again  
to receive.

The water did not choose  
where to go.

It reached everything.

And as it moved,  
the whole landscape  
seemed to breathe.

Nothing was forced.  
Nothing was broken.

Yet what was false  
did not remain.

And I understood:

There is a quiet movement  
that restores all things—  
not by striving,  
but by revealing  
what is true.

#### Living the Vision

There are moments when something feels unsettled.

When you sense  
that not everything within you  
is aligned or clear.

You may feel the urge  
to fix it quickly,  
to push away what feels wrong,  
to make things right  
through effort.

But there may be  
another movement  
already present.

You may notice it  
in a soft awareness—  
a clarity that arises  
without strain.

It does not accuse.  
It does not rush.

It simply shows  
what is ready to be released.

And as you stay with it,  
even gently,  
something begins to shift.

Not everything changes at once.

But what is no longer needed  
starts to loosen.

What is true  
begins to stand more clearly.

You are not asked  
to force this process.

Only to remain open  
to what is being revealed.

There is a restoring  
that happens quietly—  
like water moving through the earth—  
bringing life  
where it is welcomed.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** You renew within me  
>
> **Exhale:** I rest in what is true
