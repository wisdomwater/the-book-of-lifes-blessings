## 79 - The Wings of Quiet Knowing

> *What is hidden is gently held*  
> *and unfolds in its time*

I saw a figure  
standing beneath a widening sky.

From its shoulders,  
wings were lifted—  
broad and luminous,  
resting close,  
yet ready.

One curved softly along the back,  
like a shelter  
over what had been.

Another opened across the chest,  
folded inward and outward at once—  
as if holding  
both what is known  
and what is still becoming.

The wings did not move quickly.

They breathed.

Within them,  
there was a quiet depth—  
like pages unseen,  
like words not yet spoken.

Nothing was hidden in fear.

Everything was held  
in care.

Light touched the edges—  
not to reveal all at once,  
but to soften  
what was ready.

And I saw  
that the wings did not conceal  
to keep away—

but to protect  
what was still ripening.

Below them,  
there was peace.

No urgency.  
No reaching beyond the moment.

Only a steady presence  
that trusted  
the unfolding.

And the wings remained lifted—  
not closed,  
not distant—  
but open  
to the quiet work of time.

#### Living the Vision

You long to understand—

to see clearly,  
to know fully,  
to have what feels unfinished  
come into view.

And yet,  
you may notice  
that some things remain  
just out of reach.

Not withheld—  
but not yet opened.

You may feel this  
in your own life—  
in questions that linger,  
in meanings that shift slowly,  
in truths that deepen  
over time.

There is a patience  
within you  
that knows how to live here.

You may sense it  
when you stop striving  
to gather everything at once.

When you allow  
what is present  
to be enough for now.

Something in you  
already trusts  
that what is needed  
will come into light.

Not all at once.

But faithfully.

You are not behind.

You are not missing something.

You are being carried  
within a wisdom  
that unfolds  
as you are ready to receive it.

And gently,  
you may begin to rest  
within what is given—  
while what is hidden  
ripens in peace.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** I rest in what is given  
>
> **Exhale:** I trust what will unfold
