## 66 - The Hidden Garden of Love

> *You are not sustained by being seen*  
> *You are sustained by what is real*

I saw a wide place  
where the ground was heavy—  
soft,  
uncertain,  
unable to hold firm weight.

Footsteps sank into it.  
Movements became slow,  
burdened,  
as though each step  
was caught in something unseen.

There was a constant reaching—  
a desire to rise,  
to be lifted,  
to be noticed above the surface.

But the more it reached,  
the deeper it seemed to settle.

Then,  
at the edge of this place,  
I saw something different.

A quiet garden—  
hidden,  
almost overlooked.

Its soil was firm,  
dark and rich.

Nothing there called attention to itself.  
Nothing strained to be admired.

Yet life was everywhere.

Roots spread deep.  
Leaves opened slowly.  
Growth moved in silence.

No one stood watching.

No voice announced  
what was unfolding.

And still,  
it flourished.

Not because it was seen—  
but because it was rooted  
in what could sustain it.

And I saw  
that this hidden place  
was not lesser.

It was free.

#### Living the Vision

You feel the pull  
to be seen.

To have what you do  
noticed,  
affirmed,  
recognized.

It can feel like  
that is what gives your actions  
their meaning.

But you may also notice  
how this pull can weigh on you—  
how it makes you aware  
of how you appear,  
how you are received,  
how you are measured.

There is another way  
that feels quieter.

A turning inward—  
not away from others,  
but toward what is true  
beneath the surface.

You may try it gently.

To do something good  
without sharing it.

To let a moment  
remain unspoken.

To allow your life  
to be rooted  
where only God sees.

At first,  
it may feel like something is missing.

But then,  
something steadier  
begins to grow.

A freedom  
from needing to perform.

A quiet strength  
that does not depend  
on being noticed.

You are not lost  
when you are unseen.

You are being rooted  
in something deeper.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Root me in what is true  
>
> **Exhale:** Let hidden love grow
