## 187 - The Restored Trust

> *What is returned becomes light again*  
> *What is made right begins to rest*

I saw a quiet place  
where many paths met.

The ground held traces  
of what had passed—  
footsteps crossing,  
lines that had once taken  
more than they gave.

The air felt unsettled,  
as if something  
had been disturbed.

Then,  
one by one,  
small offerings were set down.

Nothing grand.  
Nothing forced.

Just a gentle returning—  
a placing back  
where something had been taken.

And as this happened,  
the ground began to soften.

The lines faded.  
The weight lifted.

A calmness moved through the space,  
like a breath  
that had been waiting to return.

Nearby,  
a well of clear water  
stood open.

Its surface was still,  
reflecting light  
without distortion.

Those who came near  
did not draw from it quickly.

They paused.

They looked.

And in that quiet,  
something within them  
began to settle.

Nothing demanded.  
Nothing accused.

Only a steady presence  
that welcomed  
what had come back into place.

And I understood:

There is a peace  
that returns  
when what was taken  
is gently restored.

#### Living the Vision

You sense a quiet imbalance.

Something given too little.  
Something taken too quickly.

It may not always be clear at first.  
It may come as a feeling—  
a subtle unrest  
that does not fully leave.

You may want to move past it.  
To let time cover it.

But there may be  
another invitation  
waiting gently within.

You may notice it  
in a simple awareness—  
a sense of what could be returned,  
what could be acknowledged,  
what could be made right.

Not perfectly.  
Not completely.

But truly.

This is not about weight.  
It is about freedom.

When something is restored,  
even in a small way,  
you may feel it—  
a lightness,  
a clearing,  
a quiet return of trust.

You are not asked  
to undo everything at once.

Only to follow  
what is being shown.

And to trust  
that each small act of honesty  
opens the way  
for peace to live again.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Teach me what is true  
>
> **Exhale:** Help me restore in love
