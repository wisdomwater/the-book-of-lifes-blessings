## 178 - The Clear Window

> *What is watched with care remains clear*  
> *What is received in stillness stays true*

I saw a quiet dwelling  
resting in soft morning light.

Its windows were wide and open,  
facing the horizon.

A gentle mist moved across the land,  
drifting slowly,  
touching everything it passed.

At times,  
it brushed against the glass—  
soft,  
almost unnoticed.

But the windows remained clear.

From within,  
a steady presence watched—  
not tense,  
not guarded by force,  
but awake.

Light filled the room,  
meeting whatever came  
without being dimmed.

The mist did not enter.  
It did not settle.

It passed on,  
leaving no trace.

And through the clear glass,  
the world could be seen  
as it was—  
open,  
unclouded,  
whole.

There was no struggle  
between the light and the mist.

Only a quiet clarity  
that remained.

And I understood:

What is gently watched  
does not become confused.

It is held  
in a deeper seeing.

#### Living the Vision

There are many things  
that pass through your awareness.

Words you hear.  
Images you see.  
Impressions that come and go  
without asking.

Some feel light.  
Some feel unsettled.

You may notice  
how quickly they move—  
how easily they can shape  
what you think or feel.

And yet,  
there is a place within you  
that does not have to follow.

A quiet awareness  
that can remain still  
as things pass by.

You may sense it  
in a pause—  
when you notice  
without immediately reacting.

In that moment,  
something stays clear.

You are not asked  
to block everything out.

Only to remain present  
to what enters,  
without letting it take hold.

This is a gentle guarding—  
not closing,  
but seeing.

And as you rest in that seeing,  
your words,  
your responses,  
your inner world  
begin to carry  
a quiet steadiness.

Not perfect.

But clear enough  
to remain at peace.
