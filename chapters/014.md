## 14 — The Fire That Gives Life

> *The mystery is not empty*  
> *It is alive with Presence*

A vast radiance opened—  
not distant,  
but surrounding.

It had no edge.

No boundary  
where it began  
or where it ended.

And yet,  
it was not still.

It moved—  
like a living warmth  
carrying quiet intention  
through everything it touched.

Not consuming.  
Not destroying.

But sustaining.

Like a fire that does not burn away,  
but gives life to what it holds.

Within it,  
all things found their place.

Breath moved.  
Light formed.  
Sound arose  
and returned again  
to silence.

Nothing stood apart from it.

And yet,  
its depths remained hidden—  
not withheld,  
but too full  
to be contained.

The radiance did not explain itself.

It did not reveal all it carried.

It simply remained—  
present,  
active,  
alive beyond knowing.

And within its warmth,  
something quiet unfolded—

a gentle refining,  
like gold resting in flame,  
not losing itself,  
but becoming clear.

#### Living the Vision

There are moments when life feels uncertain.

When you sense  
how little can be fully known—  
about where you have come from,  
or where things are leading.

You may feel the desire  
to reach for clarity,  
to define what cannot yet be seen.

And yet,  
there is a presence  
already holding you  
within that unknown.

Not distant.  
Not silent.

Alive.

You may notice it  
in the quiet steadiness  
beneath changing circumstances—  
a warmth that remains  
even when understanding does not.

At times,  
this presence may feel like refinement.

An inner stirring.  
A gentle pressure  
that asks you to release  
what no longer belongs.

Not as loss—  
but as clearing.

You are not being undone.

You are being made clear.

You do not need to grasp  
the whole of what is happening.

Only to remain  
within the warmth  
that is already sustaining you.

The mystery is not against you.

It is the space  
where life is still unfolding.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Living fire within me  
>  
> **Exhale:** Sustain and make me clear
