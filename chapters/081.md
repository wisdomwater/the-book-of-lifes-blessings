## 81 - The Light Within Knowing

> *Understanding rests in a deeper light*  
> *and becomes clear without striving*

I saw wide wings  
held open in stillness.

They did not move  
to escape or ascend—  
but to shelter  
what was quietly held within.

Inside each wing,  
there was a book.

Not closed,  
not fully open—  
but resting  
as if waiting to be read  
in its proper time.

Light touched the pages gently.

On one side,  
a soft green shimmer—  
like life just beginning to rise.

Beside it,  
a pale silver glow—  
clear,  
reflective,  
like still water at dawn.

On the other side,  
a deep blue radiance—  
quiet and vast.

And next to it,  
a warm gold—  
steady,  
full,  
unchanging.

The pages did not speak in words.

They revealed through presence.

Nothing rushed to be understood.

Everything was held  
in a patient clarity.

And I saw  
that the light did not come  
from the pages alone—

but from what held them.

A wisdom  
greater than thought,  
yet moving through it—  
gently illuminating  
what was ready to be seen.

#### Living the Vision

There are times when  
you reach for understanding—

when you want to see clearly,  
to make sense of what is before you.

And yet,  
you may notice  
how easily the mind  
begins to strain—  
trying to grasp  
what cannot be hurried.

But there is another way  
of knowing  
that feels quieter.

You may sense it  
when you pause—  
when you allow a question  
to remain open  
without needing to close it.

Something in you  
is able to rest here.

To observe.  
To receive.  
To let insight come  
instead of forcing it.

You may find  
that understanding  
begins to unfold  
in small, unexpected ways—  
through experience,  
through stillness,  
through a gentle inner clarity.

This is not the loss of reason.

It is its deepening.

Held within something  
more spacious,  
more patient,  
more alive.

And over time,  
what once felt distant  
begins to feel known—  
not only in thought,  
but within your whole being.
