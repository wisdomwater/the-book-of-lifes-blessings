## Day 197 - The Elements Within

> *As the elements move through the body, the virtues move through the soul. The Holy Spirit makes the human being a living harmony of heaven and earth.*

The devil deceived the first human being through the serpent.

Among all creatures, he found the serpent most ready for deceit. He feared to approach humanity openly, so he chose the serpent as his instrument.

The serpent moves between two realms—sometimes in water, sometimes upon the earth. In both it is cunning. It glides in water. It creeps upon the ground. It hides and strikes with venom.

So the devil used the serpent’s craft to persuade humanity to lose joy and confidence in God.

When the human being tasted the fruit, the knowledge of evil entered through its sweetness, and sin was recognized in its juice.

Yet this evil would later be washed away in the water of baptism.

The work of God, which is humanity, will not end.

And the works of humanity will not vanish.

The works that reach toward God will shine in heaven. The works that turn toward the devil will remain in punishment, unless they are erased by clear repentance.

For as the four elements are within the human body, so the virtues of God are within the good soul.

Fire shows the soul the flame of the Holy Spirit—an inextinguishable fire that gives all good things, ignites all good things, raises all good things, and teaches all good things.

Air shows faith, the banner of victory. Faith teaches the straight path and waters the mind with hope.

Water shows how the soul abandons vice and longs for virtue. Through water, the Holy Spirit cleanses indignity and perfects His gifts.

Earth teaches the human being to offer the flesh to God and abandon the luxury of the world.

Thus the soul is nourished by celestial virtues and produces seeds of good works.

God created humanity so that heavenly works might overcome earthly things.

The human being carries the world within the body, yet is called to become the standard of heavenly harmony.

#### What This Vision Means

This vision sees the human being as a meeting place of creation and spirit.

The elements are not merely outside us. Fire, air, water, and earth become mirrors of the soul’s life with God.

Fire becomes the Holy Spirit’s flame.

Air becomes faith and hope.

Water becomes cleansing, prophecy, wisdom, and the flowing life of virtue.

Earth becomes humility, discipline, and fruitfulness.

Humanity is not rejected because it is embodied. Rather, the body becomes a sign of the spiritual life. The task is not to escape creation, but to let the virtues order creation within us.

#### Living This Vision

You are not divided between body and soul as though one were holy and the other useless.

Your breathing, hunger, tears, labor, and fatigue can all become teachers.

What fire needs to be purified in you?

What breath of faith needs to return?

What water needs to soften what has hardened?

What earthly work can bear good fruit?

Let the Spirit make your whole life—body and soul—a harmony of God.
