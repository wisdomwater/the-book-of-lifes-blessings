## 58 — The Dawn of Reverence

> *A gentle awe is rising within you*  
> *And in that awe, you begin to see*

I saw the first light of morning  
touching the edge of the world—

soft,  
almost hidden,  
yet unmistakable.

It did not arrive all at once.

It unfolded—  
slowly,  
tenderly—  
revealing what had always been there.

The air grew still.

The ground seemed to listen.

And within this quiet,  
I felt a deep awareness—  
not of distance,  
but of nearness.

As though everything  
was being held  
within something greater.

There was no fear  
that pushed away.

Only a gentle trembling—  
a sense of wonder  
at what is  
and has always been.

And in that awareness,  
something within  
began to bow—

not in heaviness,  
but in recognition.

A quiet honoring.  
A willingness to see  
clearly.

The light continued to rise.

Not forcing,  
not overwhelming—

but inviting.

And with each moment,  
what had been unseen  
became known  
in a deeper way.

#### Living the Vision

There are moments when  
you become aware  
of something beyond yourself—

not as an idea,  
but as a presence  
you can almost feel.

It may come  
in stillness,  
in beauty,  
in a pause  
that opens unexpectedly.

And in that moment,  
you may notice  
a soft reverence forming—

a sense that your life  
is part of something  
you did not create,  
yet are deeply held within.

This is not something  
you need to produce.

It arises  
as you become attentive.

You may feel  
a quiet shift—  
a desire to move  
with more care,  
to speak with more truth,  
to live with a deeper awareness  
of what is sacred.

This is where wisdom begins—  
not in certainty,  
but in openness.

Not in control,  
but in reverence.

You can remain here,  
even briefly,  
and let it shape you  
from within.
