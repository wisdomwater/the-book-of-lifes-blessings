## Day 39 – The Lion Who Advances

> *The hidden mystery moves forward with power, breaking what once held the soul captive.*

I saw the sun advance, and with it, the lion moved.

Strong.  
Unyielding.

It did not hesitate.

As it went forward, it shattered what lay before it—  
breaking through what had once seemed unbreakable.

And I understood—this was the Word made flesh.

The hidden mystery revealed.

The Son of God, fully divine and fully human,  
moving into the world.

Where He went, the works of darkness could not remain.

Chains were broken.  
Deception unraveled.  
What held the faithful was torn away.

And when the sun turned, I saw the lion turn with it.

It did not depart.

It remained.

For this mystery does not fade.

It returns to God, yet remains in God—  
one with Him.

And in this returning, I saw judgment again.

Not harsh, but true.

The old ways were transformed.  
What had been partial became complete.

The old law gave way to the new.

And the lion roared.

Not in anger—  
but in joy.

A sound that carried life.

A proclamation of something new:

Baptism.  
Regeneration.  
Spirit and water flowing together.

A new beginning.

#### What This Vision Means

This vision centers on the Incarnation as an active, transformative force.

The lion represents Christ in His power—not only to reveal truth, but to break what binds the soul. His coming is not passive; it disrupts darkness and liberates those who are held.

The movement of the sun reflects both revelation and return. Christ enters the world, transforms it, and remains united with the Father, continuing His work through judgment and renewal.

The transformation from the old law to the new signifies a shift from external observance to inner transformation.

The roar of the lion symbolizes joy and victory—expressed through baptism, which marks a new life rooted in the Spirit.

#### Living This Vision

There may be things in your life that feel unbreakable.

Patterns.  
Fears.  
Habits that seem to hold you in place.

This vision reminds you:  
they are not beyond reach.

There is a power that moves gently, yet decisively—  
able to break what binds without destroying you.

You do not have to force this change.

You only need to remain open to it.

To allow something new to begin.

Even now.
