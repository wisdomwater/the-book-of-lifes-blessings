## 203 - The Undivided Song

> *Love gathered wholly becomes radiant*  
> *The heart made single learns a deeper music*

I saw a vast and gentle brightness  
like clear air after rain—  
pure,  
alive,  
filled with quiet light.

A soft breeze moved within it,  
carrying the fragrance of blossoms,  
warm earth,  
and living herbs in summer fields.

The air itself  
seemed to sing.

And within that radiance  
stood luminous souls  
clothed in white garments  
woven through with threads of gold.

Their clothing shimmered softly,  
breathing sweetness into the air  
as though love itself  
had become fragrance.

Upon their heads  
rested crowns braided with lilies and roses,  
bright with hidden beauty.

And when a voice  
like the tenderness of the Lamb  
moved through the light,  
a music awakened within them.

Not loud.  
Not forced.

A song unlike any other—  
clear,  
whole,  
filled with joy too deep for speech.

Only they could sing it.

And yet all who heard it  
were filled with gladness,  
as though the music carried  
the peace of belonging completely to love.

And I understood:

There is a purity  
that is not emptiness,  
but wholeness—  
a heart no longer scattered,  
but gathered into one song.

#### Living the Vision

There are times when your love  
feels divided.

Part of you longs for what is true,  
while another part  
is pulled in many directions—  
toward distraction,  
fear,  
approval,  
or restless desire.

And this scattering  
can leave the heart tired.

But beneath it,  
there may be  
a quieter longing.

A desire not merely to want many things,  
but to belong deeply  
to what is life-giving.

You may notice it  
in moments of stillness—  
when something within you  
wants simplicity again.

Not a smaller life.  
A more whole one.

You are not asked  
to become flawless.

Only to let love  
gather you gently.

A single honest prayer.  
A faithful act.  
A quiet return  
to what matters most.

These begin to draw  
the scattered parts together.

And as they do,  
something within you  
grows clearer—  
a deeper harmony,  
a quieter joy,  
a song that rises naturally  
when the heart rests  
undivided in love.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Gather my heart in love  
>
> **Exhale:** Let my life become song
