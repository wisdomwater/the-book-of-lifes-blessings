## 26 — The Threefold Way of Light

> *You are held through every movement*  
> *Even your struggle is being guided*

I heard a soft sounding again—

not distant,  
but near,  
like breath  
moving through open space.

From it came three currents—

each distinct,  
yet flowing  
from the same source.

One moved like warmth—  
steady,  
clear,  
without resistance.

It did not waver.  
It simply was—  
like a quiet flame  
resting in its own light.

Another moved like wind—  
shifting,  
turning,  
full of motion.

It carried both ease  
and unrest—  
like a path  
still unfolding.

The third was gentle—  
a soft radiance  
that seemed to hold  
both the warmth  
and the movement  
within itself.

It did not divide.

It gathered.

And I saw  
that all three  
belonged together—

not separate ways,  
but one unfolding  
seen from different places.

Nothing was outside  
this holding.

The stillness,  
the movement,  
the quiet light—

all were carried  
within a single presence  
that did not strain  
to bring them into harmony.

They already were.

#### Living the Vision

There are times when your life feels steady—

clear,  
aligned,  
at rest within itself.

And there are times  
when it feels like movement—

uncertain,  
shifting,  
full of effort.

You may wonder  
which one is right.

Which one you are meant to be in.

But there is something deeper  
than both.

You may begin to notice  
that even in the movement,  
you are still held.

That even in the uncertainty,  
there is a quiet presence  
not separate from you.

You are not moving  
in and out of what is true.

You are moving  
within it.

And as you allow this—  
even gently—  
something begins to soften.

The need to resolve everything,  
to arrive somewhere final,  
begins to loosen.

You are already  
within the unfolding.

And in time,  
what feels divided  
will be gathered—  
not by force,  
but by a deeper unity  
that has always been present.
