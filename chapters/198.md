## 198 - The Radiance Beyond Seeing

> *What the heart longs for already draws near*  
> *The joy of God shines beyond all words*

I saw a vast radiance  
stretching beyond sight.

It was not harsh or blinding,  
though its brightness was deeper  
than anything I had ever known.

I could not look into it fully.

It was like seeing light reflected upon water—  
clear,  
shimmering,  
hinting at a beauty  
too great to contain.

And within that radiance,  
there was joy.

Not loud,  
not restless—  
but full,  
gentle,  
overflowing with peace.

The air itself seemed alive  
with sweetness,  
like the fragrance of blossoms  
carried on warm wind after rain.

And I saw many figures  
moving within the light.

Some wore garments bright as morning.  
Others shone like gold in sunlight.

All were radiant in different ways,  
yet none stood apart from the others.

There was no striving there.  
No fear of being forgotten.

Each soul rested  
as though it had finally come home.

And I understood:

The joy prepared by God  
cannot be fully spoken—  
yet even a distant glimpse  
awakens the heart  
to keep walking toward the light.

#### Living the Vision

You wonder whether  
your small faithfulness matters.

A prayer whispered quietly.  
A turning back after failure.  
An ordinary kindness  
offered without recognition.

It can seem too small  
to carry eternal meaning.

And yet,  
something within you  
still longs for goodness.

Still turns toward light.

You may notice  
how even the faintest glimpse of beauty—  
a peaceful moment,  
a sudden gratitude,  
a deep stillness—  
awakens something ancient in you.

A remembering.

You were not made  
only for struggle and weariness.

You were made  
for joy.

Not shallow happiness,  
but a fullness  
that the soul already senses  
without fully seeing.

You are not asked  
to understand it completely.

Only to keep turning toward it.

Every sincere return,  
every quiet act of love,  
every step toward mercy  
is already shaping you  
for a deeper radiance.

And nothing offered to God  
is forgotten.

> **Inhale:** Draw me toward Your light
