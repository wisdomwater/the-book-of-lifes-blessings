## 204 - The Veiled Radiance

> *What is hidden is not absent*  
> *The soul is drawn by joys it cannot yet contain*

I saw a radiant light  
opening before me—  
clear,  
living,  
filled with quiet power.

And within that brightness  
there appeared another light,  
deeper still.

It stretched beyond all measure,  
vast as eternity,  
soft as dawn through mist,  
yet brighter than the sun upon water.

I longed to look fully into it.

But the radiance grew so immense  
that my sight could not hold it.

The light did not reject me.

It simply exceeded  
what I could yet receive.

And before the deepest brightness  
there rested something like a veil—  
thin as breath,  
gentle as a curtain stirred by wind.

Beyond it,  
I sensed immeasurable joy.

Not empty hiddenness,  
but fullness waiting quietly,  
like music heard faintly from another room.

My heart leaned toward it  
without fear.

Wonder filled the space  
where understanding ended.

And I understood:

There are joys  
already surrounding us  
that the soul can only glimpse  
for now.

Yet even the glimpse  
is enough  
to awaken longing.

#### Living the Vision

Something beautiful  
touches you unexpectedly.

A stillness in prayer.  
A sudden peace.  
A sense of being held  
without knowing how.

And then it passes.

You may wonder  
if it was only imagination,  
only emotion,  
only a fleeting feeling.

But perhaps something real  
was quietly near.

You are not meant  
to grasp these moments tightly.

You are meant  
to receive them gently.

There is wisdom  
in allowing mystery  
to remain mystery.

Not everything beautiful  
can be explained.

Some things are given  
only as a taste—  
a soft opening,  
a glimpse through the veil,  
a reminder that life is larger  
than what can presently be seen.

You do not need  
to force clarity.

The longing itself  
is already part of the gift.

It stretches the soul  
toward a deeper joy,  
teaching you to trust  
what cannot yet be fully held.

And in that quiet trust,  
something within you  
begins to widen—  
making room  
for a radiance  
still unfolding.
