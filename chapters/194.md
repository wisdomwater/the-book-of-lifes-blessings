## Day 194 - No More Tears

> *When the old world passes away, sorrow will no longer rule the heart. The saints will see openly what they once sought through longing.*

Since no new vice appeared, I understood that after the end of the world the devil will no longer stir up filthy vices to deceive humanity.

The world will no longer exist as it did before.

The vices will no longer grow.

No one will have to resist them in the old way, for evil spirits will no longer inspire them. There will be no more temporal testing. Their memory will be erased from the earth, where human beings will no longer live in the fragile way of flesh and time.

All things will have passed into eternity.

Then the instability and weariness of the world will be finished.

There will be no more terrors as there were when human beings lived in a temporary world, surrounded by danger.

And John, the chosen one, showed this in his heavenly vision:

“God will wipe away every tear from their eyes. Death will be no more. Mourning, crying, and pain will be no more, for the former things have passed away.”

And I understood.

God will remove every wound of sin that caused tears to flow from the conscience of the saints. He will bring them into eternity like pure clay shaped for living life, as the first human was created.

Death will no longer come as it does now.

Childhood will not end in youth, youth in old age, and old age in death.

The saints will no longer live in tearful exile.

They will not wait for another life, because they will already possess the life that does not grow weary.

They will no longer cry out without knowing the end of their longing.

They will no longer search for hidden answers.

For they will see the glory of God openly and forever.

Pain will no longer torment them.

The craving to possess and the fear of loss will be gone.

No temporal power will trouble them.

They will be safe from every evil, for the former world will have passed away.

Whoever longs for life should take these words and place them deep within the heart.

#### What This Vision Means

This reading is a vision of final consolation.

The struggle against vice belongs to the world of time. As long as humanity lives within the fragile conditions of the present world, temptation, grief, danger, and resistance remain.

But eternity brings another condition altogether.

No more tears does not mean that suffering never mattered. It means suffering will no longer have power to define reality. The wounds of conscience will be healed. The exile will end. The longing for God will become sight.

This is not escape from creation, but creation brought into fullness.

The saints do not merely stop suffering. They behold God.

#### Living This Vision

Some sorrows cannot be solved quickly.

Some questions remain hidden.

Some griefs keep returning.

This vision does not ask us to deny that. It gives us a horizon.

The ache we feel now is not the final truth of our lives.

There will be a life without fear of loss, without the exhaustion of striving, without the hiddenness that makes faith so difficult.

Until then, we carry the promise in the heart.

God will wipe every tear.
