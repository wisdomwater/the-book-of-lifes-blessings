## 194 - The End of Exile

> *What has longed in darkness will awaken in light*  
> *What has wept will finally rest*

I saw a wide horizon  
opening beyond all distance.

The air was clear—  
without heaviness,  
without shadow,  
without the ache of waiting.

And across that open brightness,  
there was a quiet peace  
resting over everything.

Nothing strained there.

No voice cried out in sorrow.  
No heart searched in fear.

The long weariness  
that once moved through the world  
had fallen away  
like a garment no longer needed.

And those who walked in that light  
were unburdened.

Not because they had forgotten suffering,  
but because suffering  
no longer held them.

Their faces were open,  
their eyes clear,  
as though they were seeing fully  
what they had once only hoped for.

No distance remained  
between longing and fulfillment.

The searching had ended.

All things rested  
within a living nearness  
that filled every space with joy.

And I understood:

There is a life  
where nothing beloved  
can be lost—  
where every tear  
has become light.

#### Living the Vision

There are sorrows  
that seem to stay close.

Losses that return quietly.  
Questions that remain unanswered.  
Longings that do not fully leave.

You may wonder  
if the heart was meant  
to carry so much waiting.

And yet,  
something within you  
continues to hope.

Even gently.  
Even faintly.

You may notice it  
in moments when beauty touches you unexpectedly—  
a stillness,  
a kindness,  
a sudden peace  
that feels larger than the moment itself.

These are not the fullness.

But they are reminders.

The ache you carry  
is not meaningless.

It points beyond itself.

You are not made only for striving,  
or grieving,  
or enduring.

There is a deeper life  
already calling you—  
a life where love remains,  
where nothing true is lost,  
where the heart no longer fears separation.

Until then,  
you do not have to force certainty.

Only carry the promise gently:

that what is unfinished now  
will one day rest  
in wholeness.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Hold my longing  
>
> **Exhale:** Bring me into peace
