## 39 — The Strength That Moves Forward

> *What is bound can be gently undone*  
> *What is true moves forward without fear*

I saw a warm light  
rising—

steady,  
clear,  
carrying a quiet strength.

Within it,  
something moved—

not with urgency,  
but with certainty.

It did not hesitate.

Where it passed,  
what had felt fixed  
began to loosen—

not shattered in violence,  
but opening  
as if it had never been meant  
to hold so tightly.

There was a presence  
within the light—

strong,  
grounded,  
unafraid.

It did not struggle  
against what it met.

It simply continued.

And what could not remain  
in that presence  
fell away.

Not lost—  
but released.

I felt a deep assurance—

that nothing true  
was being taken,  
only what had bound  
was being lifted.

And as the light moved,  
something new began—

a freshness,  
like water  
touching dry ground.

A quiet beginning  
rising from within  
what had been held.

The movement did not end.

It remained—

alive,  
present,  
still unfolding.

#### Living the Vision

There are moments when  
something in your life  
feels fixed—

a pattern,  
a fear,  
a way of being  
that seems difficult to change.

You may have tried  
to move past it,  
to push through it,  
to leave it behind.

And yet,  
it remains.

But there is also  
a quieter movement—

one that does not force,  
yet does not stop.

You may notice it  
in small openings—

a new response,  
a different thought,  
a soft willingness  
to see things another way.

This is not you  
trying harder.

It is something deeper  
moving within you.

You do not need  
to break yourself free.

You only need  
to remain open  
to what is already  
gently undoing  
what binds.

And as you do,  
something new begins—

not all at once,  
but truly.
