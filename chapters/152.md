## 152 - Clothed in Quiet Light

> *You are not meant to be wrapped in worry*  
> *You are gently clothed in something deeper*

I saw a soft twilight  
resting over the earth—  
not dark,  
but dim with quiet.

In that hush,  
a figure stood.

Its hands were full—  
holding small, shifting things  
that slipped and gathered again.

The air around it  
was thick with movement—  
currents of thought,  
currents of care,  
circling without rest.

Yet just beyond this motion,  
there was a light.

It did not press inward.  
It did not demand.

It waited.

Like a garment  
laid gently nearby—  
woven of stillness,  
threaded with peace.

And as the figure paused,  
even slightly,  
something softened.

The hands loosened.  
Not all at once—  
but enough.

One thing fell free.

And in that small opening,  
the light drew closer—  
not to take anything away,  
but to cover,  
to hold,  
to settle.

The air grew clearer.

The weight  
was no longer the center.

And though the world  
remained as it was,  
the figure stood differently—  
no longer wrapped in motion,  
but quietly clothed  
in something unseen  
and sure.

#### Living the Vision

Your mind fills  
with what must be done.

Details gather.  
Responsibilities press in.  
One thought leads to another—  
until it feels  
as though you are holding  
everything at once.

You may notice  
how natural this feels—  
how easily concern  
becomes your covering.

And yet,  
something in you  
longs for another way.

Not to abandon what matters,  
but to be held  
while you carry it.

You may begin  
very simply.

Not by releasing everything,  
but by loosening  
your hold  
on one small thing.

Let it rest,  
even for a moment.

In that space,  
you may sense  
a quiet presence—  
steady,  
unrushed,  
unafraid of what you carry.

You are not asked  
to manage the world alone.

There is a deeper clothing  
being offered—  
one that does not tighten,  
but eases.

And you can receive it  
without leaving your life behind.

#### Breath Prayer

> **Inhale:** Clothe me in Your peace  
>
> **Exhale:** I release what I hold
